## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Support / Protection)

* **Role:** **Protector / Control Support**
* **Opener:** *Mage Armor* prepped → start with *Protection from Evil and Good* on frontline, triggering **Arcane Ward**.
* **Flow:** maintain midline; throw *Web* to break charges; pop *Shield* or *Absorb Elements* for clutch defense, replenishing the ward.
* **Team Play:** bodyguard the face or healer; *Misty Step* to intercept or peel threats.
* **Finisher:** lock down routes with *Arcane Lock* (site play) or freeze stragglers with *Ray of Frost* into kill zones.

**Effect Flavor:** hexagonal panes of glassy light ripple out from her prism; impacts shatter into harmless silver dust.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A noble adjunct trained for court duels, Lucia chose the field unit after shielding a street choir from an Echo riot.
She now contracts as an “arcane bodyguard,” insuring people, not palaces.

* **Lifestyle:** sparse apartment; wall of chalk sigils, tea tins labeled like spell slots.
* **Income:** escort retainers + hazard bonuses + ward commissions.
* **Relationships:** mutual respect with Ilyana Swanmere (#231); friendly rivalry with Iria Fenwright (#228).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Barriers don’t mean isolation; they’re promises. She builds them so others can reach each other safely.
