## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a blacksmith’s daughter, Rin joined the monks to temper strength into purpose.
Her draconic blood amplifies her ki, making her both feared and admired as a teacher who can split stone with her palms.

* **Lifestyle:** spartan; lives on strong tea and sparring.
* **Income:** dojo wages + bounty contracts.
* **Relationships:** rival-friend with Miyara Caelwen (#211), protector of several orphans under monastery care.
