## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Mounted Threading / Pike Wall)

* **Role:** **frontline controller–escort / lane shaper**
* **Opener:** **Mounted** shallow charge; **Precision Attack** to secure the **lance** tag on the lead brute, then **Trip** or **Push** to collapse the knot without crushing into the crowd.
* **Flow:** **Dismount (5 ft)** when the span narrows; form a **pike wall** with allies; **Action Surge** to chain **Trip + shove** and open three clean paces.
* **Finisher:** shoulder bind and angled push—foes slide off the safe rail line like rain.

**Damage Flavor:** beads race down the lance’s etched lines; when he sets the pike, rain splits around the point like it respects geometry.
