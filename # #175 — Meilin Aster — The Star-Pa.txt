## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Healer/Support Controller**
* **Opener:** **Archer Form** + *Faerie Fire* for advantage; follow with **Guiding Bolt** (from Star Map) to set up ally burst.
* **Flow:** Swap to **Chalice Form** during triage — every healing spell splashes extra healing.
* **Finisher:** **Moonbeam** corrals foes; **Thorn Whip** drags stragglers back through the light.

**Damage Flavor:** pale star-lines snap into hexagonal panes that refract each spell like small constellations.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Heiress of a minor survey house that mapped star-echoes above Valeronnes.
She joined the Aegis Circle to turn family astronomy into practical protection.

* **Lifestyle:** tidy studio loft lined with star charts; tea labeled by constellation.
* **Income:** stipend + hazard pay for night surveys.
* **Relationships:** research buddy to Ilyana Varelle; debate partner to Cyris D’Velan; swaps field notes with Alen Viremont.

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Truth is a lens: her duty is to polish it, not bend it.
