## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Speed Duelist / Mobile Support**
* **Tactics:** Uses *Step of the Wind* to reposition and strike from new angles; opens with ranged *Kensei Shot*, finishes with *Flurry of Blows*.
* **Signature Opener:** Petals swirl from her sash, and she whispers a mantra before striking.
* **Finisher:** Spins midair with a flash of pink steel and landing bow.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Raised in a monastery hidden by blooming illusions, she left to protect the temple’s border paths from Echo distortions. Dreams of one day becoming a “petal master” — a guardian of both blade and harmony.

* **Lifestyle:** Ascetic simplicity, sleeps under flower canopies.
* **Income:** Temple stipend + ritual offerings.
* **Relationships:** Student of Mina Soryu; sparring partner of Talia Renhaven.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Acts with pure intention; believes every strike must bring balance, never vengeance.
