## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Raised in a small riverside monastery, Ren learned early that words often failed where fists could mediate peace.
He now runs a tiny dojo for orphaned youth while taking adventuring jobs to fund it.

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist, quiet, drinks tea or sake after fights.
* **Income:** teaching + paid combat escorts.
* **Relationships:** rivals turned allies with a paladin named Kaen Vos.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes discipline creates freedom — every strike must mean restraint, not dominance.
