## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Velna once taught arcane harmonics in the cathedral-college of Gravemere.
When an echo surge consumed her students, she bound their souls into harmonic bells that now accompany her spells.

* **Lifestyle:** quiet, nocturnal; keeps a candlelit studio lined with chimes that ring softly when spirits approach.
* **Income:** cathedral grants, exorcism contracts.
* **Relationships:** mentor of Eryndor (#267), long-time colleague of Mireia (#266).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Sees necromancy as a conversation with grief, not an act of defiance.
She believes every ghost only lingers because no one has yet listened to their song.

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** silence—the true, absolute kind where even echoes die.
* **Weaknesses:** overextends sorcery points maintaining simultaneous summons.
* **Strengths:** precise control of necrotic resonance and emotional composure.
