## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born in the servant quarters of a duelist academy, Silven was raised polishing blades and imitating masters until he became one—though prettier, quieter, and much more distracting.

* **Lifestyle:** clean and minimal; lives behind the training halls.
* **Income:** noble tuition, private duels, protection contracts.
