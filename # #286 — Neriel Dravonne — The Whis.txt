Once a noble dancer in a Drow court, she escaped when her patron tried to turn her into a living puppet.
Now she sells information between the surface and the underdark.

* **Lifestyle:** opulent but paranoid—bed always in the middle of the room.
* **Income:** spy contracts, noble secrets.
* **Relationships:** flirts with Vynessa (#284) during negotiations; respects Kaelen (#283).
