## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Control / Push-Pull Blaster / Face**
* **Opener:** *Fog Cloud* to break enemy lines of sight → step to vantage with *Misty Step*.
* **Flow:** *Eldritch Blast* + **Repelling** to knock foes into hazards; maintain *Blur* if pressured.
* **Finisher:** lock a priority target with *Hold Person* for party crits; layer **Genie’s Wrath** on the decisive hit.

**Damage Flavor:** each blast forms a spinning water sigil that collapses into a cold snap and a backward surge like a receding wave.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

An apprentice scribe who proved that compassion and precision can coexist in pact law.
She left the desk to “field-test” better clauses that protect common folk from predatory spirits.

* **Lifestyle:** tidy water-level loft; sleeps to wave-sound recordings.
* **Income:** court retainers + adventuring shares.
* **Relationships:** compares notes with Satsuki Renwei (#210); friendly tea debates with Aelina (#209).
