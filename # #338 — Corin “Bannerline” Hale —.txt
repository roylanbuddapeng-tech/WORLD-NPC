## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Line Captain / Protective Support)

* **Role:** **frontline anchor / guard support**
* **Opener:** plant shield and **Protection**-cover squishies; call formations with the pennon.
* **Flow:** trade space with spear/longsword; pop **Rallying Cry** with **Second Wind** when the team dips, then **Action Surge** to flip momentum.
* **Finisher:** shield bind → short thrust to rail seam; calm order, not chaos.

**Damage Flavor:** when he calls a rally, the glass-inset on the shield pulses soft gold; the team’s breath syncs to his.
