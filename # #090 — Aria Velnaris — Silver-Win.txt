## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A courier raised in a cliffside outpost, Aria learned flight before speech. When the Wind Verge Guard needed someone reckless enough to dive through lightning storms, she volunteered before the sentence ended.

* **Lifestyle:** early riser, thrives outdoors.
* **Income:** bounties, courier jobs, survival contests.
* **Relationships:** drinking buddy with Lioren Vale, sparring partner of Jin Hoshikawa.
