## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Kaelith was trained in the Venom Market under Skarnov’s guild of quiet killers.
Unlike most, he experiments with toxins not just to kill — but to *communicate* through the body’s reactions.

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist, quiet apartment above a perfume shop.
* **Income:** assassination contracts, alchemical sales, silent protection work.
* **Relationships:** tangled with Selune (#295); rivalry and mutual respect.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes morality dilutes efficiency. Mercy is acceptable only when paid for.
