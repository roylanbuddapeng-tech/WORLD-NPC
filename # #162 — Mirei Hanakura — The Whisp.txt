## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Duelist / Mobility DPS**
* **Tactics:** Always isolates targets, weaving between melee and theatrical flourish. Uses *Fancy Footwork* to dance out of reach after each strike.
* **Signature Opener:** Spins dagger midair, steps into stance — “One breath. One bloom.”
* **Finisher:** Smoke petals burst; she appears behind the foe as the last petal falls.

**Damage Flavor:** flashes of rose-red energy trail behind her slashes, petals scattering with every parry.
