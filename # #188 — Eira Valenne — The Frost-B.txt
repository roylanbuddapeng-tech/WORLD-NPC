## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Eira is a former champion duelist who turned instructor after losing her mentor to an Echo duel gone wrong.
She now guides younger fighters to master precision without pride.

* **Lifestyle:** minimal, elegant, with ritual tea each dawn.
* **Income:** teaching + dueling contracts.
* **Relationships:** mentor-figure to Mika Lioren; sometimes teases Rinelle about “swinging too hard.”

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics
