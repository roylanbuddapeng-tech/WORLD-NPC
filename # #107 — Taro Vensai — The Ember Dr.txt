## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a tribal guardian, Taro lost his people to an Echo firestorm. Now, he wanders — equal parts protector and penitent pilgrim.
He aids villages, hunts aberrations, and teaches the art of “controlled rage.”

* **Lifestyle:** ascetic, quiet; drinks only during festivals.
* **Income:** bounty contracts, exorcism pay, blacksmith work.
* **Relationships:** occasional sparring partner with Elias Corven; respected by Mira Solenne.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

He believes rage isn’t evil — it’s sacred fire. What matters is what you choose to burn.
