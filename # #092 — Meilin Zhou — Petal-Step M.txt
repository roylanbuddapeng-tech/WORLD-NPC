## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born a merchant’s daughter, Meilin left city life to train in the Veil after an Echo storm destroyed her hometown bridge. She found peace through movement — and anxiety whenever praised.

* **Lifestyle:** simple and disciplined; trains at dawn, collects herbs at dusk.
* **Income:** temple donations and courier fees.
* **Relationships:** Treats Aria Velnaris as an admired “big sister”; looks up to Selene Arctis as divine perfection.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes violence should bloom only as self-defense; healing follows every strike.
