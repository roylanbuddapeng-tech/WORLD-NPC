## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Backline Support)

* **Role:** **Backline Buffer / Crowd Control / Safe Reposition**
* **Opener:** **Mantle of Inspiration** to temp-HP and reposition allies safely; overlay *Faerie Fire* for advantage.
* **Flow:** *Calm Emotions* to neutralize mobs; *Healing Word* to pick up downs at range; *Dissonant Whispers* to peel threats off the line.
* **Finisher:** **Suggestion** to end a standoff without blood; *Vicious Mockery* to stagger damage dealers.

**Flavor:** her chorus casts thin ribbons of light that tether to allies’ shoulders; when she lifts a hand, the ribbons gently tug them to safety.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Raised to present court announcements, Yuna learned early that a steady voice can turn a riot into a conversation.
She joined the Chorus to bring that steadiness to streets, not just stages.

* **Lifestyle:** tidy studio with mirrored wall and soft lantern strings.
* **Income:** performance fees, escort retainers, relief-order stipends.
* **Relationships:** often paired with Rei Kazemori (#243) on peace details; duet partner with Serena Lioren (#235).
