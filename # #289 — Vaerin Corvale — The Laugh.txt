## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Poison Controller / Charmer Assassin)

* **Role:** **infiltration & social elimination**
* **Opener:** charm → close distance → needle jab handshake.
* **Flow:** feign vulnerability; attack when they breathe her perfume.
* **Finisher:** *Assassinate* with rapier to heart, whispering apology.

**Damage Flavor:** victims’ eyes glaze over as she kisses their cheek — the scent of roses the last thing they know.

## 💬 Personality

* **Traits:** composed, flirtatious, enjoys control through affection.
* **Ideal:** Power — “A smile is sharper than any blade.”
* **Bond:** secretly protects orphans of Valeronnes with her pay.
* **Flaw:** overestimates her ability to read people; guilt burns slowly.
* **Quirk:** tastes each new poison on her own tongue first.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born into nobility, trained in dance and court diplomacy; discovered poisons when she sought a way to survive politics.
Now lives among the shadows of her old house, protecting the weak while killing for coin.

* **Lifestyle:** elegant, perfumed halls, deadly beauty in every motion.
* **Income:** noble patrons, assassination commissions.
* **Relationships:** admirer of Vynessa (#284), silent competitor to Ryn (#285).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Sees morality as balance through pain and pleasure. “I kill them kindly, so they die believing in beauty.”

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** losing her composure—her charm is her mask.
* **Weaknesses:** guilt-driven; overuses poison near innocents.
* **Strengths:** mastery of seduction, toxin design, and acting.
