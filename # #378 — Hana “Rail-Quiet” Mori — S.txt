## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Telekinetic Lane-Pin)

* **Role:** **frontline defender / micro-control**
* **Opener:** chalks a seam → **Protective Field** on first ally hit to settle nerves; **spear+shield** tag to hold aggro.
* **Flow:** nudge foes off safe lines with **Psionic Strike** force pops; **Telekinetic Movement** yoinks a stumbling civilian behind her shield; **Action Surge** to convert defense into a quick shove-and-secure turn.
* **Finisher:** short pivot, two-handed spear thrust; rain ripples follow her line like stitched thread.

**Damage Flavor:** every **Psionic Strike** sheds a silver bead that shoots along the spear and bursts in a quiet ring.
