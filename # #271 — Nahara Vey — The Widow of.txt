## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Nahara once worked as a grave tender for an abandoned village, learning that fungus could carry memory as well as decay.
When she sang to the soil, voices began to answer.

* **Lifestyle:** nomadic, sleeps in root hollows.
* **Income:** sells antidotes, toxins, and ritual reagents.
* **Relationships:** occasional ally to Kaien (#269); rival of Velna (#268).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She believes morality is irrelevant to the cycle — death is just digestion in a larger body.
