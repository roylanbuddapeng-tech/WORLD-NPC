## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A street performer raised in the flame carnival, Rin learned to steal applause before he ever stole gold.
He adventures for thrill, not money — “It’s all just part of the show.”

* **Lifestyle:** bright clothes, sleeps under tents or beside warm forges.
* **Income:** performance tips + light heists.
* **Relationships:** flirts shamelessly with both genders; rivals Kaia for “best rooftop landing.”
