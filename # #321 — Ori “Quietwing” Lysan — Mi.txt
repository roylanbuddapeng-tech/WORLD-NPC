## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Silent Skirmisher / Telepathic Support)

* **Role:** **infiltrator / comms hub / precise striker**
* **Opener:** **Psychic Whispers** to link team → **Hide** → silent thrown blade to drop sentry.
* **Flow:** kite between cover; bolster tricky checks with **Psi-Bolstered Knack**; maintain telepathic callouts.
* **Finisher:** step-in cross of twin psychic shards to the solar plexus; leaves only a sigh of cold air.

**Damage Flavor:** blades appear as thin violet feathers of light; each hit sounds like a page turning.

## 💬 Personality

* **Traits:** gentle, introspective, smiles with his eyes; compliments come out like lullabies.
* **Ideal:** Solace — “If a secret hurts, carry it softer.”
* **Bond:** keeps night letters from rescued couriers, tied with silver thread.
* **Flaw:** avoids burdening others; will shoulder too much risk alone.
* **Quirk:** hums a three-note motif before difficult locks—his “open, please.”

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Archive orphan taught to read footfalls before letters. A psionic spark gave him words without sound; the Quiet Hands shaped that into duty. He delivers truths people can’t afford to say out loud.

* **Lifestyle:** attic nook above scriptorium; window plants, folded letters, one teacup.
* **Income:** courier retainers, hazard pay on Echo nights, cipher commissions.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

He believes secrecy is care when used to shield the vulnerable—and cruelty when used to hide harm.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** losing his voice to Echo-silence (no telepathy through the hush).
* **Weaknesses:** light frame; if cornered without cover, fragile.
* **Strengths:** flawless quiet strikes, team telepathy, clutch-skill reliability.

## 🤫 Secrets & Plot Hooks

* **Secret:** one letter he carries is addressed to himself—from a future he doesn’t remember.
* **Hooks:**

  1. **Feather Cipher:** his three-note motif appears carved into Echo stone—who taught the walls his song?
  2. **Moon Beak:** an assassin steals his telepathic link, speaking through it to frame the party.
  3. **The Letter That Won’t Open:** a sealed missive burns cold unless opened where “the city forgets its name.”
