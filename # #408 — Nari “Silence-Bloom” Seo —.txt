## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Mercy as a Weapon)

* **Role:** **mobile controller / surgical finisher / field medic (twisted)**
* **Opener:** **Patient Defense** through arrows, step inside, **Hand of Harm** on the loudest threat—“anesthetic” by necrosis.
* **Flow:** herd targets along glass seams with feints; **Deflect Missiles** to keep lanes safe; pivot to **Hand of Healing** only for assets she wants alive.
* **Finisher:** thumb at carotid → tonfa hook → single palm strike; rain beads into a clean circle that fades like a closing iris.

**Damage Flavor:** when Hand of Harm lands, a bruise flowers in sea-teal rings, as if ink blooms under skin.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Runs a spotless one-room clinic that takes “night cases.” Records are immaculate, handwriting like printed type. A shelf of white paper flowers bears dates—“patients discharged to silence.”

* **Lifestyle:** austere cot, sterilizer, tea kettle, jar for “Dot.”
* **Income:** quiet donations, hazard retainers, off-book contracts.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Pain is noise. **Silence cures.** She measures compassion in millimeters of blade.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** **Severance spans** where rain makes no sound—she can’t “hear” pain to target it.
* **Weaknesses:** no armor/shield; limited burst vs heavy armor; ki-gated.
* **Strengths:** **AC 17/Speed 40**, self-sustain (**Hand of Healing**), precise burst (**Hand of Harm + Flurry**), elite mobility/control.

## 💞 Interactions (Behavioral Style)

* **How she makes people blush:** fixes your bandage with gentle, absurdly close focus, whispering, “Breathe on two… good.”
* **How she teases:** taps your pulse with a cool fingertip; smiles when it syncs with rainfall.

## 🤫 Secrets & Plot Hooks

1. **White Blossoms Ledger:** every paper flower marks a “mercy discharge.” Several dates match missing-person reports—follow the trail.
2. **Ink Bloom:** a Severance broker wants her Hand-of-Harm “samples.” If she accepts, Echo-ink begins staining living walls.
3. **The Unfinished Patient:** a powerful foe she once spared “to suffer properly” returns—force her to choose mercy or massacre.
