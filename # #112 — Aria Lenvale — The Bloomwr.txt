## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Former adventurer turned healer, Aria left battlefield chaos behind to teach others peace through nature.
Still, she treats wounds too deftly for someone who’s “retired.”

* **Lifestyle:** tranquil; sleeps surrounded by bioluminescent flora.
* **Income:** donations, herbal medicine trade, blessings from travelers.
* **Relationships:** close to Selene Vaurelle (old comrade); surrogate mentor to Miyu Hanari.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics
