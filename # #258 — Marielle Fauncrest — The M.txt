## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Marielle once served as a royal botanist but turned to druidic ways after her son was lost to Severance infection. She seeks to “heal what kingdoms forget.”

* **Lifestyle:** woodland cottage near a moonlit pond; open-door policy for lost adventurers.
* **Income:** herb trade + spiritual healing + bounty share from corrupted beasts.
* **Relationships:** godmother to Liora (#256); mentor to several Circle initiates.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Life and decay are one melody. She believes nurture is stronger than conquest.
