## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once the High Priest of Ashfire, Kaien defied the church by fusing divine and necrotic rites, believing death and light were inseparable.
He was exiled after resurrecting an entire village’s dead to finish one final hymn.

* **Lifestyle:** lives alone in the catacombs; lights hundreds of candles before sleeping.
* **Income:** funerary contracts, cleansing rituals.
* **Relationships:** respected by Mireia (#266), regarded as “blasphemer” by the living clergy.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

He views good and evil as irrelevant to the final truth of death — he serves balance, not faith.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** becoming unable to distinguish prayer from necromancy.
* **Weaknesses:** physically weary; burns his life force to empower divine necrosis.
* **Strengths:** iron will, balanced control between radiant and necrotic power.
