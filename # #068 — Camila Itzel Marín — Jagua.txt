## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Child of a caravan cook and a river-guard, Camila grew up running deliveries through markets. A rooftop race with thieves revealed her jaguar reflexes — and a career.

* **Lifestyle:** Sleeps light, lives on street food and sunfruit.
* **Income:** Scout contracts, bounty splits, courier bonuses.
* **Relationships:** Spars with Tsubasa Renjou for “sky-ground” races; friendly rivalry with Jae-Lan Sereth.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

If a job hurts the powerless, she flips it or walks. “Gold buys bread; it shouldn’t buy fear.”

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Strengths:** Terrain mastery, battlefield control via maneuvers, rescue acrobatics.
