## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Frontline Support / Aura Protector / Controller**
* **Tactics:** Opens **Twilight Sanctuary** to stabilize allies; tags priority targets with **Guiding Bolt** then holds lines with **Shield of Faith**.
* **Signature Opener:** whispers a night-hymn → mantle blooms with silver motes → allies steadied by calm light.
* **Finisher:** **Moonbeam** dropped over chokepoints while she advances under a raised shield.

**Damage Flavor:** radiant light flows like falling moon-petals; each strike leaves a hush, as if the night is listening.

## 💬 Personality

* **Traits:** Gentle authority, patient mentor, quietly teasing with those she trusts.
* **Ideal:** Mercy — “Night shouldn’t frighten the innocent.”
* **Bond:** Guards choir children who rehearse by the canals.
* **Flaw:** Overprotective; steps into danger she doesn’t need to.
* **Quirk:** Counts heartbeats before each prayer; keeps the tempo slow and steady.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Former orphan-warden who learned to read the city by sound and shadow.
Now she leads a small patrol of volunteer choristers who follow her hymns into the dark.

* **Lifestyle:** modest quarters above a lantern chapel; warm tea, folded blankets for strays.
* **Income:** city stipend + donations from families she’s protected.
* **Relationships:** respects Lyra Thalassara; trading blessings with Rhea Corvalen; playful debates with Lucien Valeir about “beauty vs. comfort.”

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She believes **safety is a kind of music** — you feel it in the body before you hear it in the air.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** A night where no hymn can be heard (Echo swallowing sound).
* **Weaknesses:** Hesitates to abandon civilians, even tactically unwise.
* **Strengths:** Superb team stability, anti-fear aura, night vision command.
