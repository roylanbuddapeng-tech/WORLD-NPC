## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Controller / Enchanter / Charmer**

Now teaches charm magic to heal minds, not enslave them.

* **Income:** potion exports, charm commissions, and rose elixir sales.
* **Relationships:** close mentor to Selene Vaelor; has complicated history with noble lord Esten Corval.
