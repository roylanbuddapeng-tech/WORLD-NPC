## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Illusion Striker / Distraction Specialist)

* **Role:** **battlefield controller / misdirection**
* **Opener:** *Disguise Self → sneak infiltration → Silent Image* of herself to mislead enemies.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Kaelia grew up performing street illusions for coin before being scouted by The Velvet Veil.
Her beauty and talent made her famous—but her hands stayed quick and her knives quicker.

* **Lifestyle:** nocturnal performer, high tips, low trust.
* **Income:** performance, scams, and theft from nobles.
* **Relationships:** occasional flirt with Selune (#288), sparring rival with Vaerin (#289).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes lies can protect better than truth ever could. “Reality is boring—fantasy kills cleaner.”

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** being truly seen and understood.
* **Weaknesses:** fragile when her illusions fail.
* **Strengths:** agility, deception, and perfect timing.
