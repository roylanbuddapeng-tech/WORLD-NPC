## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Seraphine was once a city physician before the war scattered her family.
Now she travels with a small wagon converted into a mobile clinic, her wolf ears twitching when danger nears.

* **Lifestyle:** rustic but cozy; lives by herbal tea and campfire soup.
* **Income:** donations + minor church support.
* **Relationships:** acts as an older-sister figure to Rin Kazarra (#215).
