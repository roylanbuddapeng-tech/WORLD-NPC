## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Lior grew up as a stage hand before stealing the show with his dancing.
He uses his feminine charm to get close to targets and his dagger to make sure they never forget him.

* **Lifestyle:** lavish yet carefree; sleeps on rooftops or in theatre rafters.
* **Income:** performances and “private retrieval jobs.”
* **Relationships:** playful rival to Seline Ardyn; often calls Rinelle “big sister knight.”
