## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a metalsmith’s apprentice, she struck a pact with an Efreet who admired her stubborn spirit.
Her forges now run half by hand, half by magic, and the line between creation and destruction blurs beautifully.

* **Lifestyle:** workshop-home, smells of smoke and jasmine oil.
* **Income:** artifact crafting commissions, magical weapon reforging.
* **Relationships:** sparring friend to Kaela (#218); supplier for Thalassaran scouts.

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She believes fire is divine — not because it destroys, but because it *changes* what it touches.
