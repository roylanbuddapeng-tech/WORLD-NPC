## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Illusion Support / Heist Cleric)

* **Role:** **backline support / advantage engine / stealth enabler**
* **Opener:** **Blessing of the Trickster** on party rogue → **Invoke Duplicity** to split attention.
* **Flow:** **Mirror Image** for defense; **Hold Person** on a high-value target; **Pass without Trace** to disengage cleanly.
* **Finisher:** bait with duplicate, flank with ally for advantage chain; taunt with *Command: “Kneel.”*
