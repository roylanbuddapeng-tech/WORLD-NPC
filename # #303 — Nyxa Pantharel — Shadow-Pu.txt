## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Shadow Skirmisher)

* **Role:** **ambush opener / scout controller**
* **Opener:** **Pass without Trace** on team → rooftop glide → first-turn **Dread Ambusher** triple shot.
* **Flow:** mark alpha with **Hunter’s Mark**; rotate angles every turn; swap to blade in tight alleys.
* **Finisher:** **Zephyr Strike** burst to close distance, throat-hook with claw knife, vanish into cape shadow.

**Damage Flavor:** arrows hum a low panther purr; impacts leave a brief emerald ripple in the dark.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Raised by dock runners; learned the city’s wind and stone like a second language.
A canal riot taught her that fast feet and quieter hearts save more lives than speeches.

* **Lifestyle:** sparse loft above a boathouse; ritual stretching at dusk.
* **Income:** bounties, scout retainer, hazard pay during festivals.
* **Relationships:** professional respect for Lyra’s rangers; rivalry with Sae’ra (#299) in rooftop races.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Predators hunt; guardians counterhunt. She won’t kill for sport — only to stop the hunt from the other side.
