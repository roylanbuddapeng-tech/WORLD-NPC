## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Shadow Control / Opener)

* **Role:** **infiltration opener / silence controller / skirmisher**
* **Opener:** **Pass without Trace** on team → rooftop route → drop into **Silence** bubble to isolate a caster.
* **Flow:** **Patient Defense** to bait, counter with **Flurry**; pop **Darkness** to cut lines of fire then climb out.
* **Finisher:** short burst across a wall into throat-tap combo; whispers “lights out” as the lantern sigil snuffs.

**Damage Flavor:** impacts pull heat from the air; breath sounds briefly flatten like recording static.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Orphan runner who ferried hush-messages between stages and shrines; the Black Lantern taught her to make quiet into a weapon. She maps cities by how they swallow sound.

* **Lifestyle:** hammock loft above a bell-maker’s shop (bells kept wrapped).
* **Income:** night courier retainers, hazard pay for Echo nights.
* **Relationships:** rooftop race rival of Nyxa (#303); trades silent routes with Selune (#288).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Noise is kindness only when it’s laughter. Everything else is better quiet.
