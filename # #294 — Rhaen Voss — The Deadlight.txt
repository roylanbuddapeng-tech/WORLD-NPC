## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Rhaen once served as a Skarnov soldier; he turned monk after watching an entire battalion consumed by Severance.
He now guards the boundary between life and the dead rivers.

* **Lifestyle:** ascetic, lives in solitude above salt flats.
* **Income:** monastery stipend, offerings for peaceful funerals.
* **Relationships:** mentor to young monks; quietly respects Lyra (#1).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

He sees death not as punishment, but release. Killing cruelly is blasphemy; killing cleanly is mercy.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** losing emotional detachment and striking in anger.
* **Weaknesses:** cannot harm the dying easily.
* **Strengths:** unyielding calm, immense self-control, mastery of necrotic resonance.
