## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Offense-Focused)

* **Role:** **Primary Blaster / Soft Control**
* **Opener:** *Hex* on priority target → **Eldritch Blast** to pull into hazards or allies’ melee.
* **Tempo:** weave *Scorching Ray* for burst; punish pursuers with *Hellish Rebuke*.
* **Control:** *Blindness/Deafness* on enemy casters; reposition using terrain she sets ablaze.
* **Finisher:** drag boss across **Green-Flame Blade** line or into burning zones, securing **Dark One’s Blessing** temp HP.

**Damage Flavor:** every beam writes a line of burning script in the air that snaps shut on impact like a book slamming.
