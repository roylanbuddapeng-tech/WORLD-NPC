## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a historian for the Valeron Empire, Lucen turned war-mage when the Severance tore his archives apart.
He now safeguards what remains of magical history — and the memories that come with it.

* **Lifestyle:** lives inside the archive; sleeps at his desk.
* **Income:** relic restoration, spell transcription commissions.
* **Relationships:** teacher to Kael (#275); close friend to Rheon (#276).

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics
