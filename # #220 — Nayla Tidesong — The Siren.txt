## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Nayla grew up as a canal performer but learned the real audience was behind the curtains.
She now trades secrets hidden in melodies and uses her siren blood to echo truth through illusion.

* **Lifestyle:** luxurious minimalism — everything neat, scented with sea salt and jasmine.
* **Income:** performance contracts + covert information trades.
* **Relationships:** friendly rivalry with Mina Caeryn (#217).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Truth isn’t spoken—it’s performed. She reveals by rhythm, not argument.
