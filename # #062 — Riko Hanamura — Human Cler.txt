## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born in a poor district, Riko joined the temple chorus after surviving a canal collapse healed by clerics. She learned faith from gratitude, not fear. Now she travels to bring light where it’s needed most.

* **Lifestyle:** Bright and humble; always humming temple songs.
* **Income:** Donations, temple stipends, and herbal trades.
* **Relationships:** Admires Selene Ardyn; friendly rivalry with Orin Kaelis; best friend to Mina Soryu.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She believes healing is the highest form of rebellion against despair.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Failing someone she promised to save.
* **Weaknesses:** Physically fragile; naïve in matters of deceit.
* **Strengths:** Overflowing empathy; unwavering courage.
