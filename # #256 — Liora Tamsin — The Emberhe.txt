## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born into luxury, Liora traded silk gowns for smoke-stained gloves. She runs errands for the Arcana Syndicate, summoning flame spirits to clear ruins and “accidentally” set tavern signs on fire.

* **Lifestyle:** messy but warm; her tent smells of spice and singed parchment.
* **Income:** commission-based jobs, “hazard pay” from guild contracts.
* **Relationships:** best friend to Kaien (#255) — teasingly calls him “Professor Deathlight.”

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Fire’s purpose is to illuminate and consume — she just prefers when it does both stylishly.
