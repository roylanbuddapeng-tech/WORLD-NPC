## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Support Focus)

* **Role:** **Party Buffer / Control / Off-Healer**
* **Opener:** **Unicorn Spirit Totem** → *Faerie Fire* for advantage, then **Goodberry** prep between fights.
* **Flow:** *Entangle* or *Spike Growth* to shape terrain; *Pass without Trace* for stealth missions.
* **Heals & Cushions:** *Cure Wounds* enhanced by **Unicorn** aura; stabilizes with berries.
* **Finisher:** swap to **Hawk Spirit** to feed reaction **Help** for the party’s decisive burst.

**Effect Flavor:** translucent animal spirits (bear, hawk, unicorn) bloom from pressed leaf sigils; air smells of rain and cedar.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Raised on the border of industry and woodland, Hana learned to translate between lumber crews and the forest.
She joined the Verdant Veil to keep roads safe without turning forests into scars.

* **Lifestyle:** cabin with a rooftop herb garden; brews berry tea nightly.
* **Income:** guide fees + healing shares + rescue bounties.
* **Relationships:** trades salves with Talia Otterwell (#232); sings in Ilyana’s (#231) peace vigils.
