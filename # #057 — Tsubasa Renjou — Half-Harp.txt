## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born from a highland ranger and a harpy envoy, Tsubasa grew up half in the sky and half on the ground. She became the best courier on the Windspire’s edge—known for never losing a message, even in Echo storms.

* **Lifestyle:** Adventurous and unrestrained; drinks, jokes, and naps wherever she lands.
* **Income:** Guild courier contracts and monster bounties.
* **Relationships:** Treats Riko Hanamura as her “kid sis.” Friendly rivalry with Renji Arvalen.
