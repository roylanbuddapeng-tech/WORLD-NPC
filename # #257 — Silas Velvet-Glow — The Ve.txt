## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Offense / Summoner-lite)

* **Role:** **Blaster / Soft Controller / Familiar Harrier**
* **Opener:** **Mirror Image** → **Hex** main target → **Eldritch Blast** with positioning from familiar.
* **Burst:** **Scorching Ray** to delete priority threats (sweetly counting “one, two, three—kiss!”).
* **Close-up:** **Burning Hands** if cornered; the imp distracts or stings casters.
* **Sustain:** last-hit minions to trigger **Dark One’s Blessing** temp HP.

**Effect Flavor:** beams unfurl like lipstick-red ribbons that smack foes with sizzling kisses; the air smells faintly of warm spice.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Runaway salon-performer who traded patronage for a **Fiend’s contract** that promised “freedom with fireworks.”
He works as a courier-face for the Ember Syndicate, weaponizing charm and heat to keep allies alive.

* **Lifestyle:** perfumed loft with velvet drapes and too many mirrors.
* **Income:** escort retainer + tips + performance nights.
* **Relationships:** shamelessly flirts with Liora (#256); owes Vesper (#254) a rooftop dare-night.
