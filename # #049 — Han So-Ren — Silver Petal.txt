## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

So-Ren grew up in Valeronnes’s artisan quarter, apprenticed under a perfumer who smuggled healing tonics disguised as luxury scents. She merged art and chemistry into a single philosophy: fragrance as power.

* **Lifestyle:** Playful chaos; her workshop smells divine but is always half-burnt.
* **Income:** Atelier commission + personal orders + guild stipend.
* **Relationships:** Best friends with Kaena Mistral; friendly rivalry with Liora Vaentis in “healing vs chemistry.”

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes that joy and comfort are valid forms of healing. “If I can make someone smile or smell good while saving them, I’ve done my job.”

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Losing her sense of smell; a potion exploding mid-market.
* **Weaknesses:** Impulsive; vulnerable in melee.
* **Strengths:** Inventive thinker, adaptable in chaos, charismatic.

“รุ่นเพื่อน” tone — easy banter, teasing warmth, chemistry puns galore.

## 🤫 Secrets & Plot Hooks

* **Secret:** Her signature elixir “Silver Bloom” uses Echo-charged petals — potentially addictive.
* **Hooks:**

  1. **The Fractured Formula:** Retrieve stolen Silver Bloom before it spreads through the markets.
  2. **Scent of the Lost:** Help her distill a memory from the perfume of a missing friend.
  3. **Burning the Atelier:** Contain a runaway elemental in her lab before it consumes the entire street.
