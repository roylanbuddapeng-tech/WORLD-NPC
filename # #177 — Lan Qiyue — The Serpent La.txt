## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Frontline Controller / Off-Healer**
* **Opener:** *Bless* → **Net** a priority target → **Nature’s Wrath** to lock down bruisers.
* **Flow:** Maintains **Shield of Faith** on the squishiest ally, lashes with **whip (reach)** to punish movers, smites when a setup guarantees advantage.
* **Finisher:** Whip yank into ally’s strike lane → **Wrathful Smite** to break morale.

**Damage Flavor:** whip-trails glimmer like liquid moonlight; when she smites, teal vines spiral up the lash before impact.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Former chorus-warden who turned paladin after an Echo riot on her watch.
She learned the whip from a river-keeper lineage that “sings through rope and light.”

* **Lifestyle:** spartan, dawn prayers by the water; keeps a ledger of saved names.
* **Income:** city stipend + contract bonuses.
* **Relationships:** mutual respect with Lyra Thalassara; playful mentor energy toward Sumiya; measured debates with Cassian on “order vs. grace.”

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Justice should feel like rescue to the innocent and like consequence to the wicked. She designs every fight to end with the most survivors.
