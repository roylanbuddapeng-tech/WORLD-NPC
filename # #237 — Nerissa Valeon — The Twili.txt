## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Nerissa was once the youngest twilight cantor of the Valeronnes court before abandoning privilege to tend to war-torn refugees.
Her nightly masses became known for “bringing dreams instead of sermons.”

* **Lifestyle:** quiet convent cell filled with starlight lamps and children’s drawings.
* **Income:** church stipend, private donations, blessing rites for nobles.
* **Relationships:** long-standing mentor to Mariel Crestveil (#234); spiritual counselor to Serena Lioren (#235).
