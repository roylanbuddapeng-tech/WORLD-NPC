## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Kaoru grew up among performers and thieves in Thalassara’s undercity. He found that charm could open more locks than steel. Now he trades whispers and smiles, collecting secrets like jewels.

* **Lifestyle:** Luxurious but transient — soft beds, scented candles, and escape routes.
* **Income:** Espionage pay + stage performance tips + blackmail (rarely used).
* **Relationships:** Flirts shamelessly with Meilin Arha; mentor-brother to Rin Kaelis (much to Rin’s annoyance).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes kindness is a choice, lies are survival, and seduction is art.

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Being seen as powerless beneath his charm.
* **Weaknesses:** Fragile self-image; distractible when flustered.
* **Strengths:** Quick thinking, agility, master of emotional manipulation.

## 💞 Interactions (Behavioral Style)**

“Femboy” tone — light, airy voice with playful cadence. He leans close when speaking, his words half-whisper, half-laughter.

* **Secret:** His “perfume” contains a diluted charm pheromone from his tiger lineage — illegal under Thalassaran law.
* **Hooks:**

  1. **The Amber Heist:** Help Kaoru retrieve a stolen charm vial before it’s weaponized.
  2. **Dance of the Fangs:** Escort him through a noble gala infiltration.
  3. **Echo of the Heart:** Investigate why his reflection in mirrors doesn’t match his movement.
