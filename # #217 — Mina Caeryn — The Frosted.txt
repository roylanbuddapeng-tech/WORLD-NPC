## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Mina was a novice who discovered her connection to celestial power during a near-drowning accident.
She now balances study and practice, writing every discovery meticulously in her rune journal.

* **Lifestyle:** diligent but clumsy; studies till dawn.
* **Income:** scholarship stipend + tutor aid from senior clerics.
* **Relationships:** looks up to Lyra Thalassara (#1) as role model; has a quiet crush on a rival apprentice.
