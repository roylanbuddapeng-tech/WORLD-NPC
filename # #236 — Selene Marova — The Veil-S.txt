## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born among Thalassaran tide-priests, Selene was trained to maintain sacred echo-balance through music and ritual.
Her serene faith hides a rebellion: she believes mercy is stronger than scripture.

* **Lifestyle:** sleeps in floating sanctum cells surrounded by candlelight.
* **Income:** temple tithe, healing donations, ceremonial performances.
* **Relationships:** long-time collaborator with Lyra Thalassara (#001); mentor to Hana Redvale (#233).

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics
