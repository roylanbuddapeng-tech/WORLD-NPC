## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a street urchin sold to a traveling troupe; his voice caught the attention of the cabaret’s mistress.
He learned to weaponize empathy, and now rules the stage with whispers and poison in song.

* **Lifestyle:** luxurious, perfumed, perfidious.
* **Income:** performances, secret deliveries, blackmail.
* **Relationships:** often seen with Vaerin (#289); rumored connection to a noble general.
