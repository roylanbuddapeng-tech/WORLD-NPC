## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Enchantment Control)

* **Role:** **Crowd Controller / Manipulator / Soft-Disable Caster**
* **Opener:** *Mirror Image* → *Charm Person* to redirect enemies.
* **Flow:** *Hold Person* to lock high-value target → *Calm Emotions* when allies break.
* **Finisher:** *Suggestion* whispered gently with her own name hidden inside the phrase.

**Damage Flavor:** never direct — always emotional; enemies stop fighting mid-motion, mesmerized by tone.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Daughter of a mirror artisan family; gifted with synesthetic empathy—she sees emotion as color.
Joined the Council to mediate disputes, but discovered how easily she could “paint peace” with suggestion.

* **Lifestyle:** refined, quiet chamber with scented glass lanterns.
* **Income:** official retainer of Council + “private persuasion consults.”
* **Relationships:** mentor to Aevius (#282); rumored to have “saved” Selyra (#281) once using Hypnotic Gaze.
