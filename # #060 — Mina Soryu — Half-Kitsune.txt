## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** Trickster / Controller / Distraction Artist
* **Tactics:** Confuses enemies with illusion clones, darts through gaps using *Misty Step*, blindsides with *Phantasmal Force*.
* **Signature Opener:** Flicks fan open, smirks — “Try to guess which one’s me.”
* **Finisher:** Cloak flares; a foxfire illusion bursts into dazzling sparks as she vanishes behind the chaos.
* **Damage Flavor:** Embers swirl like petals in her wake.

---

## 💬 Personality

* **Traits:** Witty, teasing, unpredictable but kind to the downtrodden.
* **Ideal:** Freedom through deception — “Illusion is truth’s older, more beautiful sister.”
* **Bond:** Protects the orphans of Ember Court with her stolen earnings.
* **Flaw:** Lies habitually; struggles to be sincere even with allies.
* **Quirk:** Changes her eye color mid-conversation just to confuse people.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Mina was a street magician who accidentally uncovered her fox lineage after a flare of illusion magic set the stage ablaze. She was taken in by the Ember Court, where she turned from entertainer to information broker.

* **Lifestyle:** Decadent but modest — loves wine, incense, soft lights.
* **Income:** Performance contracts + espionage + “selective donations.”
* **Relationships:** Flirts relentlessly with Kaoru Veylan; friendly rivalry with Liora Fenvale.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes lies can heal better than truths when wielded with care.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Losing control of her illusions — revealing the true fox within.
* **Weaknesses:** Emotionally evasive, poor endurance.
* **Strengths:** Creativity, adaptability, mastery of deception and disguise.

---

## 💞 Interactions (Behavioral Style)**

“Tomboy Type 1” tone — graceful yet mischievous; voice warm but laced with teasing play.

> “Oh? You’re staring… must I perform to earn your gaze?”
> If someone teases her:
> “Tch—bold one, aren’t you? Don’t tempt a fox unless you’re ready to be caught.”

When *she’s flustered*, her tail flicks violently and she hisses softly through a grin.

---

## 🤫 Secrets & Plot Hooks

* **Secret:** Her foxfire illusions drain ambient Echo energy; she’s unknowingly stabilizing the city’s resonance web.
* **Hooks:**

  1. **The Ember Masquerade:** Protect her during a noble performance where her illusions begin manifesting physically.
  2. **The Burning Tail:** Track a rogue thief using a stolen illusion mask tied to her aura.
  3. **Mirror of Ashes:** She’s framed for assassinating a noble with one of her doubles.
