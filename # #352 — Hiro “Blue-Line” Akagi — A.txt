## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Anti-Caster Escort / Clean Finisher)

* **Role:** **frontline protector–duelist / spell-interceptor**
* **Opener:** advance under rain; the instant a hostile cast cues, pop **Shield** or **Absorb Elements** to keep the lane intact.
* **Flow:** **Action Surge** to convert a stagger into a clean finish; **Weapon Bond** yoinks the sword back if disarmed in a shove.
* **Finisher:** bind guard with the rim of the shield, slip a short cut; if a fall threatens, **Feather Fall** keeps civilians breathing.

**Damage Flavor:** when he swings, a cobalt line sketches his intent; the rain seems to flow along it before breaking.

## 🤫 Secrets & Plot Hooks
