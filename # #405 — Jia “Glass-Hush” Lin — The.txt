## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Tea-house orphan; memorized footfall tempos delivering kettles on slick rails. When the Echo taught her how rain masks a cut, she became the city’s most polite disaster.

* **Lifestyle:** a broom-clean garret; teacups labeled by rainfall pattern, blades wrapped in linen.
* **Income:** contract kills, hush deliveries, “sanitation” fees from terrified shop rows.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

“Noise is a stain; stains must be lifted.” Mercy exists if silence can be kept without a corpse.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Severance spans where **rain makes no sound**—no cover, no rhythm.
* **Weaknesses:** low sustained damage vs armored squads; relies on surprise and position.
* **Strengths:** **alpha-strike crits**, world-class stealth/mobility, surgical removal of leaders.

---

## 💞 Interactions (Behavioral Style)

* **Making others blush:** pours you hot tea mid-stakeout, adjusts your grip like a partner’s waltz, whispers “good posture.”
* **How she teases:** taps your wrist with a teabag string for timing; smiles when your pulse matches the rain.

---

## 🤫 Secrets & Plot Hooks

1. **Teacup Ledger:** each cup mark equals a death; the pattern maps a future route—decode it before the next “cleaning.”
2. **Glass Saint:** a rival claims she leaves “holy” stillness; cultists start hiring her—cut the chain.
3. **Silent Spill:** on one bridge, her blade leaves **no ring**; find the anchor swallowing sound.
