## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Illusion Control / Seduction Feint)

* **Role:** **controller / misdirection opener**
* **Opener:** *Silent Image* clone walks into view while real Iselra circles under *Invisibility*.
* **Flow:** *Mirror Image* to layer defense → *Phantasmal Force* to “paint” pain into a single high-value target.
* **Finisher:** whispers at the ear from invisibility, then retreats behind a folding hall-of-mirrors corridor.

**Damage Flavor:** enemies feel heat and breath that aren’t there; her illusions taste like mint and static.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Prodigy from a glassweaver family; she reverse-engineered court illusions into portable “light-script” packets.
Now the Council hires her when a scandal needs a prettier truth.

* **Lifestyle:** luxe little loft stacked with prisms and tea tins.
* **Income:** council retainers, private “alibi theater” commissions.
* **Relationships:** trading tricks with Kaelia (#291); mentored (reluctantly) by Vynessa (#284).
