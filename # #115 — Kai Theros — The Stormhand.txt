## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born in a fishing village destroyed by the Severance tide, Kai was the only survivor rescued by monks.
He learned their ways — calm discipline over chaos.

* **Lifestyle:** ascetic; sleeps on stone slab, meditates by sea cliffs.
* **Income:** salvages Echo artifacts and sells them through temple networks.
* **Relationships:** mentee of Aria Lenvale; occasionally sparring partner to Miyu Hanari.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes violence should be as controlled as a wave — powerful but temporary.
