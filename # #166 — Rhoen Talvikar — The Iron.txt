## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Defensive bruiser / Counter-controller**
* **Opener:** Raises shield, rotates wrist — **Trip Attack** to ground the fastest enemy.
* **Flow:** Uses **Parry** and **Protection** to defend allies; follows up with flail sweeps.
* **Finisher:** Drives *Stormlash* into the ground, unleashing stored wind charge in a 10-ft burst.

**Damage Flavor:** metallic gusts swirl; sparks flicker off impact points, resembling a forge wind in motion.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born to forge workers, fought as mercenary in the early Severance conflicts.
After injury, became a smith-trainer but still roams for commissions and apprenticeships.

* **Lifestyle:** simple, disciplined; keeps a forge-fire burning wherever he stays.
* **Income:** smithing work + combat contracts.
* **Relationships:** Mentor to Riven Caelthorn; distant respect from Cassian Veldt; fond of tea with Nerissa’s clerics.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes discipline is freedom. A well-made weapon should never taste blood without purpose.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Losing control of the “wind core” built into his chest — it hums dangerously when angered.
* **Weaknesses:** Wounds reopen if he exerts magic resonance too long.
* **Strengths:** Incredible endurance, tactical precision, and mastery of defensive combat.
