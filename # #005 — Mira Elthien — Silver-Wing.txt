## 📜 Background & Lifestyle**

* **Background:** Born to a seamstress and a bard. Her Thalassaran feather lineage manifested as a soft humming resonance when she sang. Studied under the Aural Chorus, performing for nobles and protecting sacred performances from Echo corruption.
* **Lifestyle:** Elegant but humble; shares songs for food in small towns.
* **Income:** Performer’s fee, academic grant.
* **Relationships:** Correspondent of **Lyra Thalassara** (provides rhythm maps); mentor to two young singers.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics**

Believes that **music binds memory**; destroying beauty is equal to heresy. Seeks to heal discord through art, even among enemies.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths**

* **Fears:** Losing her voice to Echo distortion.
* **Weaknesses:** Overly trusting of smooth talkers.
* **Strengths:** Inspires morale, sharp perception of emotional “tones.”

---

## 💞 Interactions (Behavioral Style)**

“รุ่นพี่” tone — gentle encouragement, mixed with affectionate teasing. Places a hand on a shoulder while whispering, “Breathe—every hero needs rhythm.” Likes to make others blush with compliments disguised as advice.

---

## 🤫 Secrets & Plot Hooks**

* **Secret:** Her wings can resonate into full spectral form once per month, granting flight for 1 minute—but the sound draws Echoes.
* **Hooks:**

  1. **Wings of the Moon Song:** Escort Mira through an Echo storm to perform a cleansing hymn.
  2. **The Broken Chorus:** Investigate a silent amphitheater where music dies mid-note.
  3. **Letters to the Lost:** A stolen letter collection contains secrets of Thalassaran lineages.
