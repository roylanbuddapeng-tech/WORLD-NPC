## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Daughter of a retired storm-knight and an engineer; grew up fixing gliders and sparring with sky pirates. She joined the guild to protect air routes from Echo storms.

* **Lifestyle:** Adventurous; loves taverns with sea views.
* **Income:** Courier wages + bounty commissions.
* **Relationships:** Drinking buddy with Riven Solthyr; competitive teasing with Myra Daeven.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes you can’t control the wind — only ride it long enough to make a difference.

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Claustrophobic spaces; being grounded permanently.
* **Weaknesses:** Reckless lunges; hates authority.
* **Strengths:** Agility, courage, fast recovery from falls.

“Tomboy” tone — bold, teasing, confident. She elbows you playfully: “You’re thinking too much. Just swing.”
