## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Black-Ribbon Control)

* **Role:** **controller / pick-off finisher / skirmish caster**
* **Opener:** **Disguise Self** to get close, then pop **Darkness (Eyes of the Dark)** so she sees and they don’t; **Twinned Hold Person** snatches two targets into stillness.
* **Flow:** thread **Chill Touch** through her personal darkness to choke off heals; **Subtle Spell** on **Sleep** or **Hold Person** in tight negotiations; **Shield** when the math says “greedy.”
* **Finisher:** a soft step to the rail and a whisper—**Fire Bolt** stitches a blue-violet seam through the rain.

**Damage Flavor:** her spells leave “ink halos” in falling rain; droplets curve around her like they’re afraid to touch.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Former stagehand who learned sightlines and mic hiss. She files victims by “dB of offense,” then bakes bread at dawn as if absolution rises with the dough.

* **Lifestyle:** one-room loft—oven, black ribbons, immaculate bedroll.
* **Income:** hush retainers, “noise abatement” contracts, side tips from grateful neighbors.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Noise is cruelty; silence is mercy. Bodies are just stubborn sounds that forgot how to stop.
