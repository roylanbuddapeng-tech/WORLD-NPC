## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a prodigy singer chosen for divine resonance, Rinna blended music and healing magic.
Her voice can mend flesh or shatter shadow barriers.
She now travels as a **“Song Healer”**, carrying a tuning crystal that vibrates when nearby souls are in pain.

* **Lifestyle:** tidy, fragrant rooms filled with scrolls and windchimes.
* **Income:** temple stipend + concert donations + healing commission.
* **Relationships:** formerly mentored by Lyra Thalassara (#1); often collaborates with Nara (#247) for field support.
