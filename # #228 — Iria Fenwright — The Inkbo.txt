## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Raised in the library’s scriptorium since youth, Iria spent her life translating ancient Severance-era texts.
Her skill in rewriting magic sigils made her invaluable — but also dangerous, as her words can reshape reality itself.

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist, quiet, filled with notes and coffee cups.
* **Income:** translation contracts, spell commissions.
* **Relationships:** occasional correspondence with Evelyn (#223); mutual respect, subtle rivalry.

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics
