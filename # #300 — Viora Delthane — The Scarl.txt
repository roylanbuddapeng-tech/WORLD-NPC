## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Spectral Assassin / Control Hybrid)

* **Role:** **ambusher / necrotic controller / vanguard cleaner**

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Former temple mourner turned assassin. She joined Silent Vow after realizing that Severance echoes feed on guilt — and hers could be weaponized.

* **Lifestyle:** quiet monastery quarters near Venshar catacombs.
* **Income:** retrieval jobs for cursed artifacts, ghost negotiations.
* **Relationships:** mentor to ghost seer Lucian (unseen NPC).
