## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Raised in the echo-markets beneath Gravemere’s amphitheater, Selyra learned to move between applause and silence.
She left the syndicate once; they kept her ledger anyway. Now she “buys it back” a name at a time.

* **Lifestyle:** sparse rooftop loft; sleeps with earplugs, one eye to the moon.
* **Income:** contracts, hush-deliveries, “problem-solving.”
* **Relationships:** wary respect with Velna (#268); mutual non-aggression with Kaelrin (#279).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She won’t start wars, just end conversations. Targeted violence over spectacle; zero collateral if she can help it.
