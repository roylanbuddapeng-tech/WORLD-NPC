## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Overwatch Controller / Precision Finisher)

* **Role:** **ranged control / pick priority**
* **Opener:** climb to rail perch; **Shadow Arrow** the enemy controller to blind their reach; mark lanes with chalk for allies.
* **Flow:** **Grasping Arrow** on chargers; **Action Surge** for double-volley when the lane opens; shift perches using silk rope anchors.
* **Finisher:** bodkin into weak plates; last shot times with ally push so the bridge goes quiet.

**Damage Flavor:** arcane shots leave faint falcon-glyphs in the air; feathers of light drift and vanish.
