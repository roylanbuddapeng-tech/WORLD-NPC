## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Ward-Duelist / Gap Sealer)

* **Role:** **point defender / anti-burst protector**
* **Opener:** **Booming Blade** into shield-forward advance; pop **Shield** in response to spikes.
* **Flow:** **Absorb Elements** to punish elemental openers, **Action Surge** to convert a hold into a push.
* **Finisher:** angle step to the rail, bind weapon, and let *Booming Blade* deter retreat—either they eat thunder or the edge.

**Damage Flavor:** runes along Sealbreaker wake in silver; each parry traces a faint ring that snaps shut with the final cut.
