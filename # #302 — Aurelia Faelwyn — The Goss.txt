## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A minor glass-noble’s ward, she learned etiquette as misdirection. A fey crossing answered her envy with a pact: beauty that edits attention. Now she steers feasts, politics, and tragedies with equal grace.

* **Lifestyle:** perfume, quiet balconies, letters sealed with pressed flowers.
* **Income:** noble “consults,” backstage rescues, rumor choreography.
* **Relationships:** professional rivalry with Iselra (#293); fond of Melisara (#301)’s pranks.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Won’t force minds—she engineers choices. If you fall for the mask, she says you wanted to.
