## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Analyst Duelist / Control via Read)

* **Role:** **single-target solver / scout-controller**
* **Opener:** **Eye for Detail** to read the field → **Insightful Fighting** on the boss.
* **Flow:** maintains engagement from midline, feeds allies callouts (who’s faking, who’s feinting).
* **Finisher:** quiet thrust when the lie breaks; leaves a silver thread tied around the truth.

**Damage Flavor:** her blade glints like a lie cracking; each hit “clicks” like a lock opening.

* **Traits:** playful analyst, teasingly blunt, collects tells like souvenirs.

* **Bond:** protects backstage crew who taught her every trick.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Raised among costume-makers; learned that fabric lies beautifully.
A Severance scam took her friend—she’s been dissecting cons and killers ever since.

* **Lifestyle:** compact studio with mannequins, wigs, scent-coded files.
* **Income:** private inquiries, noble vetting, backstage security.
* **Relationships:** friendly rivalry with Sereth (#304); coffee ally with Melisara (#301).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Won’t torture truths; she courts them. Lies are tools, not homes. She dislikes zealots and lazy thinkers.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** failing to read a genuine cry for help.
* **Weaknesses:** overanalyzes affection; may miss danger in romance.
* **Strengths:** lie detection, target analysis, precision fencing.
