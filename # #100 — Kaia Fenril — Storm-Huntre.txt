## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born in Thalassara’s floating quarter, Kaia grew up wrestling tidebeasts for sport. She left home after losing her ship to an Echo storm and vowed to protect sailors from similar fates.

* **Lifestyle:** nomadic, sea-weathered; sleeps on deck hammocks.
* **Income:** bounty hunts, salvage shares, mercenary scouting.
* **Relationships:** Mentor to Ryn Kael’veris, rival-friend of Suna Velrien.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes every kill should honor the sea — waste nothing, respect the cycle of predator and prey.
