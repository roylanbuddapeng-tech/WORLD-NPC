## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Manipulator / Disruptor)

* **Role:** **Social infiltrator / fear inducer / psychic duelist**
* **Opener:** **Detect Thoughts** → identify guilt → **Words of Terror**
* **Flow:** **Rapier** feints, mid-fight whisper *Dissonant Whispers* to force panic reposition.
* **Finisher:** stab with **Psychic Blades**, steal face with **Mantle of Whispers**.

**Damage Flavor:** echoing whispers crawl from wounds, as though the victim’s secrets scream to escape.

## 💬 Personality

* **Traits:** teasing, charming, dangerously intuitive; reads people by heartbeat pauses.
* **Ideal:** Beauty — “Truth is fragile, but fear is exquisite.”
* **Bond:** her troupe of underground performers who smuggle information via songs.
* **Flaw:** cannot keep from manipulating others, even for fun.
* **Quirk:** tilts head slightly when lying—her tell, though few notice.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A former noble’s daughter who turned her charisma into a blade. She dismantled an entire spy network through stage gossip. Now she “sings for truth”—though only her own version of it.

* **Lifestyle:** luxurious but lonely; gilded balcony apartment full of mirrors.
* **Income:** noble patronage, espionage contracts, coded poetry sales.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She views emotion as both currency and weapon. “A secret told willingly is worth less than one stolen mid-kiss.”

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** silence; it means no one’s listening.
* **Weaknesses:** overly reliant on charm; distrusts genuine affection.
* **Strengths:** psychological manipulation, disguise, social dominance.
