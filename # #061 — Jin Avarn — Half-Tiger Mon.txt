## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Jin trained under Master Korran from age ten, learning that restraint is the highest form of strength. After the Echo Storm swallowed the monastery’s east wing, he began wandering to rediscover lost disciples.

* **Lifestyle:** Ascetic; eats sparingly, sleeps under open skies.
* **Income:** Modest donations from travelers and teaching martial arts to guards.
* **Relationships:** Friendly mentor to younger monks, wary ally of mercenaries.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Peace before pride, always. If forced to fight, he ensures the lesson lasts longer than the bruise.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Losing control of his beast side.
* **Weaknesses:** Dislikes killing; avoids lethal blows even when necessary.
* **Strengths:** Excellent agility, precision, self-mastery.
