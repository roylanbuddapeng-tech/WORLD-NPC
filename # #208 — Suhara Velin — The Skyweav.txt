## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A runaway apprentice who joined the Sky Relay Guild, Suhara mastered aerial casting faster than any in her cohort.
She still fears thunder despite commanding it.

* **Lifestyle:** lives in a small floating loft above Thalassara.
* **Income:** courier salary + weather control commissions.
* **Relationships:** junior partner to Lyra Thalassara (#001); often teased by Rin Verade (#205).
