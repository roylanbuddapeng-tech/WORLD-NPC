## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Eryndel grew up backstage — learning secrets of illusion and human nature from watching nobles pretend on stage.
Now she weaponizes those secrets, turning whispers into tools sharper than any knife.

* **Lifestyle:** artistic luxury, apartment above a masked tavern.
* **Income:** theater performances, spy trade, noble gossip sales.
* **Relationships:** informant for Selune (#295), old rival of Nerathine (#296).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes lies are more revealing than truth. She values expression over honesty.
