## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Support Blaster / Face / Soft Healer**
* **Opener:** *Guiding Bolt* to mark a target or *Armor of Agathys* if expecting pressure.
* **Flow:** *Eldritch Blast* with Agonizing; sprinkle **Healing Light** or *Healing Word* to stabilize allies.
* **Finisher:** *Hold Person* to set up crits or *Misty Step* into ideal line of sight for the kill shot.

**Damage Flavor:** each blast is a sliver of dawn; motes of rosy-gold light spiral, reflecting in her glasses.

She left to ensure her light served the world outside the gilded halls.

* **Income:** auxiliary stipend + adventuring split.
* **Relationships:** looks up to Seraphine Caelis (#200); studies star charts with Yuna Vareth (#201).
