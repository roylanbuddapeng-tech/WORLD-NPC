## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Frontline Protector / Damage Sponge**
* **Tactics:** Pops **Rage (Bear)** to anchor chokepoints; **Reckless Attack** only to break stalemates; body-blocks for allies.
* **Signature Opener:** Lantern shutter snaps → maul drags sparks across stone → charge.
* **Finisher:** Hammer-head twist that slams foes into the wall; soft, “Stay down.”

**Damage Flavor:** Each strike sheds ember motes that fade like cooling slag.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Former pit foreman turned Warden after leading civilians out of a collapsing shaft. Refuses promotions to stay “where the doors actually break.”

* **Lifestyle:** Plain; keeps a tidy bunk and a kettle always on.
* **Income:** Warden stipend + hazard bonuses.
* **Relationships:** Mutual respect with Draven Corvell; occasional counsel from Naru Tsukihara.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Mercy for workers, iron for raiders. He measures justice by who bleeds if he fails.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Being too slow to stop a collapse.
* **Weaknesses:** Limited ranged options; wary of magic he can’t see.
* **Strengths:** Exceptional durability (Bear Rage), battlefield control, rescue leadership.

## 💞 Interactions (Behavioral Style)

“วัยกลางคน” tone — patient, grounding.

  3. **Ash-Paw Totem:** Retrieve his stolen totem bead; without it his rage falters.
