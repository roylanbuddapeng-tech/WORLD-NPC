## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Precision Striker / Control Archer**
* **Opener:** hides, pre-channels *Grasping Arrow* to pin fast enemies.
* **Flow:** coordinates with frontliners — aims to disable, not kill first.
* **Finisher:** *Seeking Arrow* through smoke or illusion.

**Damage Flavor:** silver runes trail her shots, fading like moonlight ripples.

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist elegance.
* **Income:** teaching contract, royal stipend.

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics
