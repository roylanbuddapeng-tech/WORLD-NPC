## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Kaen was once a royal guard until a failed mission scorched his unit.
Now, he forges weapons for younger warriors, teaching that strength without craft is meaningless.

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist; sleeps near the forge.
* **Income:** smithing commissions + combat contracts.
* **Relationships:** close ally of Ren Akirato (Monk #193); sees him as a younger brother in discipline.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes the world burns to temper its heroes. Mercy, to him, is a forge’s cool water — used sparingly.
