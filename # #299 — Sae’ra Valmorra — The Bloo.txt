## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Former street thief turned secret agent, Sae’ra climbed through shadows and politics equally well.
She commands small rogue cells from rooftops, sometimes sending orders through crows that mimic her voice.

* **Lifestyle:** spartan but with expensive taste in boots.
* **Income:** guild assignments, infiltration contracts, intelligence trade.
* **Relationships:** mentor to rookie spy Eryndel (#298).
