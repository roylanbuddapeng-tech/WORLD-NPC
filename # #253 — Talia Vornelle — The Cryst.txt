## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a noble family’s tutor, Talia left the household after discovering corruption in their magical funding.
Now she works for the Eltaran Consortium, teaching adventurers “how not to explode.”

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist elegance — a home filled with neat shelves, tea aroma, and floating quills.
* **Income:** scholar salary + hazard bonus + consulting fees.
* **Relationships:** peers respect her; students fear her exams; one apprentice adores her.
