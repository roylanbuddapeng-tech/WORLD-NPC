## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Stealth Striker / Spirit Manipulator)

* **Role:** **single-target eliminator / ghost-field manipulator**
* **Opener:** from stealth — *Sneak Attack + Wails from the Grave* on nearby target.
* **Flow:** use *Cunning Action* to vanish behind cover, re-engage next turn.
* **Finisher:** throw *Specter’s Fang* into dying foe → retrieve with whisper, storing fragment.

**Damage Flavor:** faint echoing scream trails his blade swing like wind through bone chimes.

* **Flaw:** haunted by names he can’t forget.
* **Quirk:** always leaves a white flower where a target falls.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A former undertaker turned assassin after hearing the dead whisper their killers’ names.
He believes he’s not murdering—but balancing the scales.

* **Lifestyle:** solitary, lives above a mausoleum he tends himself.
* **Income:** paid in relics and oaths, rarely in coin.
* **Relationships:** occasional silent companion to Neriel (#286); rumored to have once spared a Severance priest.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Strict sense of karmic balance. Refuses contracts on innocents or the desperate. Sees assassination as release.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** spirits turning on him.
* **Weaknesses:** emotionless façade makes trust difficult.
* **Strengths:** near-perfect stealth, precision strikes, anti-ghost expertise.
