## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Support Specialist)

* **Role:** **Buff Engine / Mobility Healer / Damage Mitigation**
* **Opener:** **Emboldening Bond** on striker + caster → **Bless** if slots permit.
* **Flow:** **Balm of Peace** to weave through allies, patching them while repositioning lines.
