## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once an apprentice botanist, Mira discovered she could awaken flowers with song.
She travels helping communities rebuild nature after Severance damage.

* **Lifestyle:** simple, nature-based, always smells faintly of jasmine.
* **Income:** herbal trade, healing commissions.
* **Relationships:** close to Selene (#226), sometimes assists her moonlight rituals.
