## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Mounted Linebreaker)

* **Role:** **Frontline Charger / Aggro Keeper**
* **Opener:** mounts fast, uses **Unwavering Mark** to draw enemy fire while allies reposition.
* **Flow:** alternates between **Lance charge + Action Surge** combo and tactical dismount defense.
* **Finisher:** full gallop pierce through enemy rank using **Asterion’s Trample Assist (flavor only)**.

**Damage Flavor:** his lance splinters in light upon impact; silver shrapnel glows briefly before fading like ash.

## 💬 Personality

* **Traits:** disciplined, dry sense of humor, unwavering loyalty to comrades.
* **Ideal:** Duty — “A promise is just a command you give yourself.”
* **Bond:** his warhorse Asterion — refuses to ride another.
* **Flaw:** guilt-ridden about a failed rescue mission; avoids emotional talk.
* **Quirk:** greets everyone with “How’s your footing?” even indoors.

---

## 🧭 Common Locations

* **Region:** **The Ashen Plains**, old cavalry training grounds.
* **Threat Gradient:** **Stable → Moderate**
* **Encounter Type:** mounted patrol, ambush defense, or escort mission.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Kael was once the youngest commander of the Silver Spur, but lost half his unit to a collapsing bridge during a cavalry pursuit. Since then, he rides alone — guarding travelers and training new lancers quietly.

* **Lifestyle:** frugal, lives in stables or campgrounds.
* **Income:** escort contracts, bounties, and riding lessons for nobles’ heirs.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes order doesn’t mean control; it means **trust under pressure**. If he charges first, it’s to give others time to think.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** losing control of his mount; letting others take the hit for him.
* **Weaknesses:** predictable in open charges, hates dishonorable tactics.
* **Strengths:** mount synergy, mobility, discipline under fire.
