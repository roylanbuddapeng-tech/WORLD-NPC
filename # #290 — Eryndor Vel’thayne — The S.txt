## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a court musician of a Drow noble, he discovered his psychic talent when his song killed a man who insulted his mistress.
He turned to assassination to “compose through endings.”

* **Lifestyle:** lives beneath an abandoned amphitheater.
* **Income:** assassin’s guild commission, rare psionic contracts.
* **Relationships:** respects Kael (#287); distrusts Neriel (#286); trained Selune (#288) in silence.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes death can be graceful if delivered without hate. He calls it “the art of ending well.”

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** forgetting his lover’s melody.
* **Weaknesses:** hesitates to kill musicians or bards.
* **Strengths:** silent mobility, psychic stealth, elegance in execution.
