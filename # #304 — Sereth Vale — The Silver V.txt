* **Role:** **alpha eliminator / infiltration specialist**

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born to a family of herbalists; after the Severance War, he learned toxins faster than prayers. The Lattice recruited him for “restoring balance through select subtraction.”

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist loft, perfumed candles masking chemical smell.
* **Income:** assassination, exotic poison crafting, paid silences.
* **Relationships:** old rivalry with Miris (#272); occasional ally to clerics needing discreet work.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Doesn’t believe in good or evil—only clean and unclean deaths. Refuses to kill children or artists.

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** being poisoned unknowingly (lost control).
* **Weaknesses:** detached, lacks empathy; overanalyzes emotions.
* **Strengths:** stealth kill precision, toxin mastery, disguise adaptability.
