## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born to a lineage of weapon forgers, Isha chose the battlefield to test her own blades.
Now she forges weapons only for those she trusts.

* **Lifestyle:** modest but organized — lives above her forge.
* **Income:** weapon commissions, bounty work.
* **Relationships:** mentor to Nami Ar’Lure (training sessions by the coast); rivalry with Pyrren Blackvale.
