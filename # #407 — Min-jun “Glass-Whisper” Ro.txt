## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Whisper-Cull)

* **Role:** **controller / single-target delete via fear+psychic burst**
* **Opener:** **Disguise Self** to get close; **Hold Person** or **Dissonant Whispers** to isolate; step in with **Rapier + Psychic Blades** for the kill.
* **Flow:** keeps the scrum unstable with **Phantasmal Force** (fake hazards on glass rails), threads **Vicious Mockery** to blunt alpha hitters, retreats behind **Minor Illusion** corners.
* **Finisher:** bows, then a needle-thrust as the rain draws a hairline arrow along the seam.

**Damage Flavor:** Psychic Blades leave a brief feathered shadow in the rain where the blade wasn’t—like a second cut that only nerves feel.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once the velvet voice of a midnight revue; now a rumor-broker who files gossip like blades. His room is a miniature stage: moveable lamps, black curtains, chalked blocking marks.

* **Lifestyle:** tidy garret with birdseed tins and perfectly folded tailcoats.
* **Income:** hush retainers, “noise abatement” contracts, cut of forged playbills.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Applause is vulgar; **silence** is honest. If a person can’t hold a quiet beat, they don’t deserve the stage of the city.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Severance spans where even illusions don’t echo—no audience, no scene.
* **Weaknesses:** modest raw damage without Psychic Blades; fragile if cornered.
* **Strengths:** **hard control (Hold Person/Phantasmal Force)**, **social infiltration**, **burst finish** with Psychic Blades, elite lying.

He adjusts your collar and murmurs like a director:

## 🤫 Secrets & Plot Hooks

1. **Raven Cues:** his birds pin ribbons where the next “noise” will die—follow the flock to stop the show.
2. **Black Script:** a forged playbill schedules an assassination during a festival hush; find the stagehand with the real script.
3. **Silent Ovation:** a cult offers him a stage that eats sound (Severance tech); if he accepts, his kills become untraceable.
