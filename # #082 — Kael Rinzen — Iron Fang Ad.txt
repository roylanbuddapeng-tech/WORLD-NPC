* **Role:** **Bruiser / Disabler / Counter-Fighter**

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Kael served in the royal forges before taking vows under the Order of the Forge Flame. He now teaches both smithing and discipline, blending philosophy with metalwork.

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist, lives above the forge; meditates before dawn.
* **Income:** crafting commissions + temple support.
* **Relationships:** mentor to young monks and smiths; old friend to Diego Valcázar.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes morality is craft — shaped by patience, ruined by haste.
