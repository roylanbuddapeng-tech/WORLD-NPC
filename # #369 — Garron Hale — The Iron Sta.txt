## 🪞 Appearance (in English)

**Ethnic Theme:** Western-European / English — broad-shouldered and weathered, with the bearing of a soldier who’s survived too many campaigns.

**Height:** **188 cm**
**Weight:** **94 kg**

**Hair:** **Short**, **slicked back with silver streaks at the temples**.
**Color:** **Dark brown graying to steel**.
**Eyes:** **Deep blue**, stern but not unkind.
**Skin:** rugged tan with scars along jawline and forearms.

## 👗 Outfit

**Exposure:** **Low** (as per male standard).

Off-duty, he dons a **dark vest**, **rolled sleeves**, and **sturdy leather trousers**, still too formal for comfort.

**Palette:** steel grey / crimson / brown leather accents

**Weapons & Gear:**

* **Longsword “Vigil”** — old but perfectly balanced, carved with motto *“For Duty and Memory.”*
* **Shield**, etched with his regiment’s emblem.
* **Dagger**, **military satchel**, **rations**, **command whistle**, and **folding spyglass**.

## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Tactical Commander)

* **Role:** **Support Tank / Battlefield Leader**
* **Opener:** calls formation, then uses **Action Surge + Inspiring Surge** to let allies act immediately.
* **Flow:** keeps the front solid, absorbing pressure; commands mid-combat using concise hand signs.
* **Finisher:** “Vigil’s Sweep” — shield bash then vertical cleave, emblem glowing red.

**Damage Flavor:** blows crack with faint echo, as though a second strike follows each swing — memory of his fallen unit guiding him.

---

## 💬 Personality

* **Traits:** disciplined, quietly humorous, protective of younger warriors.
* **Ideal:** Honor — “A title’s nothing without the men who died for it.”
* **Bond:** his late commander’s daughter (now a young knight under his care).
* **Flaw:** haunted by survivor’s guilt; overprotective to a fault.
* **Quirk:** talks to his sword before battle like an old friend.

---

## 🧭 Common Locations

* **Region:** **Blackmere Keep, Southern Marches**
* **Threat Gradient:** **Stable → Siege potential**
* **Encounter Type:** fortress defense, training yard spar, command tent strategy scene.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A lifelong soldier of Blackmere, Garron rose from squire to captain by sheer endurance. He refuses noble titles, living in the barracks with his troops.

* **Lifestyle:** modest but structured; wakes before dawn, drills, eats simple meals.
* **Income:** city stipend, personal savings, and the occasional reward for valor.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

He believes leadership is service; no man should command who cannot bear the weight of others’ fear.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** losing subordinates again; becoming obsolete.
* **Weaknesses:** slow adaptability; emotionally guarded.
* **Strengths:** morale boosting, resilience, tactical acumen, loyalty.
