## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Control / Attrition Support)

* **Role:** **controller / debuffer / attrition buffer**
* **Opener:** **Silence** to suffocate casters or **Blindness/Deafness** on the boss, then **Hex + Eldritch Blast** from cover.
* **Flow:** **Armor of Agathys** for deterrence; **False Life** pre-load; send **Vell** to mark the weak-willed via Heart Sight.
* **Finisher:** lull a dying foe with *Spare the Dying*—only to question them later through contacts (RP flavor).

**Damage Flavor:** force bolts sound like a dampened bell-toll; frost of Agathys rims her veil in moon crystals.

* **Traits:** maternal poise, velvet voice, playful double meanings; never raises her tone.

* **Quirk:** taps her bell once before every spell, barely audible.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A minor house widow who refused to let grief become an end; the Conclave taught her to treat death as a conversation. She brokers peace between the living and those who won’t stop speaking.

* **Lifestyle:** quiet waterside suite with blackout curtains and bell shelves.
* **Income:** grave mediation fees, noble retainers, vow-broker commissions.
* **Relationships:** professional respect for Viora (#300); wary fascination with Rhaen (#294).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes comfort is sacred. If a beautiful lie lets the dying rest, she’ll tell it—then carry the truth for them.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** true silence in the catacombs.
* **Weaknesses:** relies on concentration; sunlight lethargy (flavor dhampir).
* **Strengths:** relentless sustain (*False Life/Agathys*), anti-caster shutdown (*Silence/Blindness*), social leverage.
