## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a monk of the Tidewardens, Vareth turned to solitary wandering after surviving a shipwreck that killed his entire crew. He now tends to drowned souls and sunken shrines.

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist; sleeps in lighthouses and ruins.
* **Income:** offerings, storm rites, coastal bounties.
* **Relationships:** mentor to seafarers; mutual respect with Lyra Thalassara (#1).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes life and death are two tides in the same sea — one should not fear the ebb, only live the flow.
