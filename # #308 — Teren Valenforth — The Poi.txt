## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Stealth Lethality / Surgical Control)

* **Role:** **Infiltration / assassination / alpha strike controller**
* **Opener:** observe 3 rounds, mix toxin → deliver **Assassinate** crit from stealth.
* **Flow:** hide using terrain; disorient foes via poison gas; follow with precise dagger thrusts.
* **Finisher:** poison mist vial shattered underfoot → silent blade to throat.

**Damage Flavor:** each hit blooms faint chemical scent—lavender, then death.

---

## 💬 Personality

* **Traits:** courteous, restrained, unnervingly calm; compliments his targets before killing them.
* **Ideal:** Precision — “Mercy is a well-measured dose.”
* **Bond:** respects competent healers; despises reckless alchemists.
* **Flaw:** detached moral code—believes chaos should be sterilized, not judged.
* **Quirk:** stirs tea clockwise exactly three times before every plan.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once court physician to a Thalassaran duke, until he learned poison removes problems faster than politics. He still treats the poor by day to balance the ledger of his conscience.

* **Lifestyle:** townhouse clinic above perfumery.
* **Income:** high-value contracts, antidote commissions.
* **Relationships:** quiet respect with Lyra (#1); mutual silence pact with Sereth (#304).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

He believes death can be a cure. Poison is mercy sharpened.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** losing fine motor control of his hands.
* **Weaknesses:** emotional detachment; underestimates “messy” emotion-driven foes.
* **Strengths:** surgical precision, master of infiltration, rapid preparation under pressure.
