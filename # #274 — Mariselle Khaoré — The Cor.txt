## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once the captain’s wife aboard the *Azure Chord*, Mariselle drowned saving her son—then was reborn by a Leviathan’s whisper. Now, she leads the Pearl Choir, guiding those who die at sea into eternal song.

* **Lifestyle:** lives in a temple beneath the tide; sleeps suspended in saltwater.
* **Income:** offerings from fishermen and nobles alike.
* **Relationships:** motherly mentor to Liora (#273); rumored past lover of the Leviathan itself.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She believes mercy is not sparing death, but granting it gently — and beautifully.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** total stillness of the sea; when waves stop, her magic dies.
* **Weaknesses:** suffers exhaustion if separated from saltwater too long.
* **Strengths:** powerful voice-based magic, long-range summoning, charisma.
