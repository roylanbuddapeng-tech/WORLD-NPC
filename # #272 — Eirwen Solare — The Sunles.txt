## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once the youngest lecturer at Solaris Arcanum, Eirwen’s theories on “necrotic ignition” (death magic through thermal equilibrium) were banned as heretical.
She now travels, burning corrupted dead before they rise again.

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist scholar; sleeps amid open books and burnt scrolls.
* **Income:** sells cremation services for cursed corpses.
* **Relationships:** rival academic to Velna (#268); occasional ally to Kaien (#269).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She sees mercy in destruction — better to burn a soul cleanly than let it rot in false resurrection.
