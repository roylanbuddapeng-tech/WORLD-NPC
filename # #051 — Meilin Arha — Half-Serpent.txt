## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Meilin was once a temple oracle before leaving to “create truth prettier than prophecy.” She channels her serpentine heritage to sense lies, blending performance with espionage.

* **Lifestyle:** Lavish — scented oils, silks, late nights.
* **Income:** Theater revenue + secret royal contracts.
* **Relationships:** Flirtatious rivalry with Jae-Lan Sereth; mentor-like toward Rin Kaelis.

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics
