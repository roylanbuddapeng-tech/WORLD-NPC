## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Support / Hybrid Frontline)

* **Role:** **Tactical Support / Defensive Buffer / Repair Medic**
* **Opener:** *Warding Bond* on ally or Steel Defender → *Heroism* → engage.
* **Flow:** Uses Defender to guard backline; fires with *Repeating Crossbow* enhanced by INT.
* **Finisher:** *Heat Metal* on enemy armor → close in with *Booming Blade* through Defender flank.

**Flavor:** her shots leave behind silver frost rings; when she casts, tiny gears spin in her irises.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born to frostsmiths, Selara rebuilt her first automaton from scraps of a collapsed bastion.
She now maintains the border guard’s machines — both steel and flesh.

* **Lifestyle:** lives in a cold apartment filled with quiet hums of gears.
* **Income:** military engineer retainer + hazard repair bonuses.
* **Relationships:** regular collaborator with Nara Veylin (#247); frequent repairer for Azure Aegis weapons.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Machines can be rebuilt — people can only be repaired if someone stays to hold the parts together.
