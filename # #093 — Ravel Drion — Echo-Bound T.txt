## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Infiltrator / Control Trickster**
* **Tactics:** Uses *Silent Image* or *Mage Hand* for distraction, hits with *Sneak Attack* from behind illusions.
* **Signature Opener:** *Disguise Self* to blend in, whispers misdirection through *Message*.

**Damage Flavor:** faint mirror shards flicker around each strike, vanishing like shattered reflections.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Ravel grew up as a servant in the mirror courts, mimicking nobles until he fooled the guards into believing he was one. He stayed, turned espionage into art, and now trades secrets like songs.

* **Lifestyle:** nocturnal, drinks only chilled wine, collects masks.
* **Income:** espionage contracts, rumor brokerage.
* **Relationships:** Rivals with Kaien Drazel, secret admirer of Jin Hoshikawa.
