## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Offense / Summoner-Lite)

* **Role:** **Skirmish Caster / Darkness Controller / Familiar Harrier**
* **Opener:** **Form of Dread** → **Darkness** centered on ring → **Devil’s Sight** lets her fight inside, blind enemies.
* **Flow:** **Hex** priority target → **Eldritch Blast** kites; **Moth** grants Help or delivers effects, scouting invisibly.
* **Finisher:** **Armor of Agathys** pre-brawl or **Cause Fear** to peel threats from allies.

**Effect Flavor:** blasts look like ribbons of ash blown by moonwind; when they strike, a bell tone rings once, very far away.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A refugee runner who made too many solo saves to count, Vesper took a pact with an Undead Queen who “hates tyrants more than death.”
Now she ferries medicine, messages, and sometimes people through nights others refuse to walk.

* **Lifestyle:** sparse loft with rope lines strung like a training maze.
* **Income:** hazard courier pay, night bounties, underbridge tips.
* **Relationships:** flirts like a dare with Rei Kazemori (#243) in shield drills; owes Serena Lioren (#235) for a healed lung.
