## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Frontline Bruiser / Breaker**
* **Opener:** **Reckless Attack** + **Rage**, charges the heaviest target to split front line.
* **Flow:** Locks down chokepoints; if swarmed → enters **Frenzy** for extra swings.
* **Finisher:** **Handaxe** throw to stop a fleeing foe, then **pounce** with cleaving overhand blow.

**Damage Flavor:** each strike sends a bronze “claw echo” through the air; dust spirals like paw-prints in storm wind.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born to a caravan guard line; learned to listen to wind and weight like a smith listens to steel.
She left a lucrative city guard post to protect the routes most people avoid.

* **Lifestyle:** sparse tent, early runs at dawn; eats like a furnace after battle.
* **Income:** escort contracts + bounty shares.
* **Relationships:** friendly rivalry with Rhoen Talvikar; soft spot for Mika Lioren’s optimism.
