## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Kaori was raised in a temple that treats martial discipline as meditation.
When the Echo tides began corrupting Thalassaran rivers, she volunteered to guard the sacred mirrors.

* **Lifestyle:** wakes before dawn, trains twice daily, enjoys spicy food and good company.
* **Income:** temple stipend, occasional bounty from thieves subdued alive.
* **Relationships:** rival and drinking partner with Jin Korran; admired by younger acolytes.
