## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born to a line of smiths but orphaned young; raised by the Foundry Guild, Kael became their youngest artificer apprentice.
His small size and quiet determination make him the forge’s heart more than its muscle.

* **Lifestyle:** modest but tidy; sleeps near the forge.
* **Income:** guild stipend, repair commissions, adventuring side work.
* **Relationships:** idolizes Riven Kaldra; friends with Aria Sunveil for moral advice.
