## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A tea-stall helper who learned to carry scalding kettles across slick planks without spilling. The Wardens noticed his balance and his knack for calming people with a single, sincere smile—and put a spear in those careful hands.

* **Lifestyle:** tidy garret with neatly folded capes, extra towels, and ribbon spools.
* **Income:** city stipend, small tips tucked into his glove after safe crossings.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

He believes **gentleness is a tactic**: kindness draws eyes, and eyes on him means fewer eyes on panic.
