## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Tsubasa was raised by a courier guild that navigated dangerous wind tunnels to deliver messages between kingdoms. She became a frontier scout after surviving a glider accident at 14.

* **Lifestyle:** Minimalist — travels light, sleeps under the stars.
* **Income:** Guild pay + bounties + courier bonuses.
* **Relationships:** Rival-friend with Draven Corvell; fond of teasing Miraen Luthar for his “fancy boots.”
