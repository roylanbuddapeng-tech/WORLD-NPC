## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A former marine scholar turned peace-cleric, Selène believes sound itself can mend souls.
She choreographs healing hymns that synchronize with tide rhythms, earning the title “Luminous Veil.”

* **Lifestyle:** lives atop a floating pavilion; owns a small library of water-based harmonics.
* **Income:** temple salary, diplomatic commissions, rare-shell trade.
* **Relationships:** colleagues with Amara Veyrienne (#240); trusted by Lyra Thalassara (#001 exemplar).
