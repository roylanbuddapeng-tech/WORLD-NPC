## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Former smith turned monk after a tragic explosion claimed his workshop.
Now teaches physical and mental forging — the art of tempering chaos.

* **Lifestyle:** spartan but disciplined; meditates by the furnace each dawn.
* **Income:** monastery stipends and smithing commissions.
* **Relationships:** mentor to novice monk Izen, former rival of Kael Vernoss.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes power exists to refine, not to dominate — mastery through patience.

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Losing control of his inner flame.
* **Weaknesses:** Emotional repression; guilt over lost apprentices.
* **Strengths:** Unyielding focus, adaptability, wisdom forged by suffering.

## 🤫 Secrets & Plot Hooks

* **Secret:** His fire ki is sustained by a shard of Severance ember fused into his chest — dormant, but unstable.
* **Hooks:**

  1. **Ember Shard Awakening:** The shard reignites during a Severance attack.
  2. **The Lost Apprentice:** A student seeks revenge, believing Renji abandoned him.
  3. **Trial of the Furnace:** PCs must earn Renji’s respect through combat and meditation.
