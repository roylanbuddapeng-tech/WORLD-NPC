## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Former sergeant of the old Skarnov guard, Draven left after disagreeing with the new regime’s brutality. He trains younger fighters and sells his blade where justice feels possible.

* **Lifestyle:** Practical; keeps a tidy tent and journal of campaigns.
* **Income:** Mercenary pay + teaching apprentices.
* **Relationships:** Mutual respect with Corin Valen; friendly rivalry with Kaena Mistral.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

He’s pragmatic, believing morality lives in the moment: “If you can save one life, do. If not, make it quick.”

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Losing another protégé to war or corruption.
* **Weaknesses:** Slower reflexes; resists magical healing (trauma-born distrust).
* **Strengths:** Resilient, dependable, skilled tactician.

---

## 💞 Interactions (Behavioral Style)**

“วัยกลางคน” tone — protective and gently teasing, often mentor-like.
He crosses his arms with a faint smirk:

> “You’ve got guts, kid. Now try keeping them inside next time.”
> When caught off-guard emotionally, he clears his throat and looks away, muttering:
> “Don’t make this awkward. I’m terrible at thank-yous.”

## 🤫 Secrets & Plot Hooks

* **Secret:** Keeps a hidden letter from his lost student, sealed with Severance glyphs — he’s afraid to read it.
* **Hooks:**

  1. **Echo on the Blade:** Help Draven uncover why his old student’s sword hums with corrupted resonance.
  2. **Mercenary’s Code:** Decide whether to side with Draven’s old company or defend a town they’re paid to sack.
  3. **The Lost Letter:** Deliver the letter to its intended heir before Draven’s past consumes him.
