## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A novice cleric raised in the Temple of Resonant Bloom, Nerine was chosen to guard the sacred lilies said to sing under moonlight.
She trains to channel divine resonance through calm emotion.

* **Lifestyle:** tranquil mornings, tea before sunrise, meditation by canals.
* **Income:** temple allowance and donations.
* **Relationships:** adores Lyra Thalassara as her senior; often brings her herbs for patrols.
