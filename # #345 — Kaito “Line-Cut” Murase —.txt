## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Control Finisher / Lane Split)

* **Role:** **frontline control / decisive finish**
* **Opener:** shield forward; **Menacing** the lead bruiser to stall a push.
* **Flow:** call a “cut line,” then **Trip Attack** the pivot target to make the lane collapse; **Precision** converts swings in the rain.
* **Finisher:** **Action Surge** once the prone/fear window opens—short, neat, quiet.

**Damage Flavor:** when a maneuver lands, rain briefly runs in straight lines off his blade before scattering.
