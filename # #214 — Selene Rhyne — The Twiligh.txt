## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Defensive Support / Vision Control**
* **Opener:** **Vigilant Blessing** on frontline ally → *Faerie Fire* to outline threats.
* **Flow:** pop **Twilight Sanctuary** to feed temp HP and cleanse fear/charm; hold lines with shield while healing.
* **Finisher:** funnel enemies through **Moonbeam** and spear them when they stagger out.

**Damage/Effect Flavor:** a hush falls within her aura; starlight drifts like dust, and footfalls sound softer.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born near the marsh lights of Moon-Fen, Selene learned that darkness can be kind if curated.
She joined the Nightward to turn that lesson into a sanctuary others can carry.

* **Lifestyle:** ascetic neatness; tea, journals, stargazing.
* **Income:** chapel stipend + hazard bonuses.
* **Relationships:** often teams with Satsuki Renwei (#210) for night investigations; traded healing practices with Ame Linfae (#206).
