## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born to a wandering minstrel and a Thalassaran scholar, Liora inherited both curiosity and stage presence. She sees every performance as a chance to heal or awaken hearts dulled by fear.

* **Lifestyle:** Nomadic but lavish; always finds fine food and music wherever she goes.
* **Income:** Concert fees + noble patronage + story commissions.
* **Relationships:** Close friend to Han So-Ren; occasional ally to Lyra Thalassara.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes beauty and art are forms of truth that transcend law.
