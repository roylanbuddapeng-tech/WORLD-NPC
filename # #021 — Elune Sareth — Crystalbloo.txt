## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

* **Background:** Child of herbalists turned warden after the Echo vines invaded her forest. Bonded with her lynx after saving it from a Severance root trap.
* **Lifestyle:** Lives in a moss hut atop a root bridge; brews crystal tonic from dew and herbs.
* **Income:** Guild stipend + commissions for rare reagents.
* **Relationships:** Occasional trading partner of Lunara Kelthis; sends reports to Saori Kitsurana’s patrols.

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Protective but pragmatic — views nature’s violence as necessary renewal.
