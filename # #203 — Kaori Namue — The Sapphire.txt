## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Kaori was raised in a temple where motion was a prayer. She found enlightenment not through meditation, but through the rhythm of kicks and breath.
Adventuring for her is an extension of faith — carrying the wind’s freedom wherever she goes.

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist — sleeps under open sky, eats sparingly.
* **Income:** temple donations + bounty shares.
* **Relationships:** mentor to novice monks, good sparring friend of Mireya Solen (#202).
