* **Role:** **deceptive striker / fear spreader / visual manipulator**
* **Opener:** **Hide in Dim Light → Rapier strike** → **Wails from the Grave** on adjacent target.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born to stage performers; learned illusion before literacy. When a noble died during his act and whispered secrets in death, he realized he could carry more than applause—he could carry memories.

* **Lifestyle:** baroque apartment above a perfume house.
* **Income:** theater wages, secret courier work, charm sales.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Lives by the creed: “If someone will die anyway, make their last moment beautiful.”
