* **Income:** Pathguard pay + festival hazard bonuses.
* **Relationships:** Big-sister rapport with Neris and Nara couriers; respects Celin’s restraint.

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Mercy is the first law; force is its shield. She’d rather break a blade than a promise.

* **Fears:** Failing to intercept harm aimed at children or novices.
