## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a diplomat’s daughter, Mireille sought a life of conviction rather than comfort.
She joined the Azure Phalanx and became its envoy to the Moon Council, blending words and steel in equal measure.

* **Lifestyle:** refined yet humble; wakes before dawn to pray and train.
* **Income:** envoy’s stipend + honor rewards.
* **Relationships:** mentor to Lyra Thalassara, confidant of Sir Nael.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes in protecting hope, not punishing failure. Her mercy is deliberate, not naive.
