## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Psionic Skirmisher)

* **Role:** **infiltrator / silent striker / comms support**
* **Opener:** **Psychic Whispers** to squad → **Hide** → thrown psychic blade to tag sentry.
* **Flow:** vaults between cover, **Psi-Bolstered Knack** to stick landing or pick locks mid-chase.
* **Finisher:** double-blade cross strike at the temple; no blood, only a violet ripple of pain.

**Damage Flavor:** blades appear as thin violet glass splinters that hum with a musical note no one else hears.
