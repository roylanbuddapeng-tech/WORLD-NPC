## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Selene was once a noble’s wife who left court life after her child’s death to serve the Moonwell Circle.
She travels offering guidance to dream-touched wanderers, and her name is whispered in lullabies across villages.

* **Lifestyle:** simple shrine-living; sleeps beside silver candles.
* **Income:** offerings and ceremonial fees.
* **Relationships:** mentor to young clerics, rumored lover of a silver dragon in human guise.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She teaches that mercy is divine but admits that light must sometimes blind to protect.
