## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A prodigy knight who defied divine command, Kaelrin was excommunicated for saving an enemy child from execution. His divine gifts curdled into necrotic fire — a mark of rebellion and guilt.

* **Lifestyle:** nomadic; sleeps near battlefields to “listen to the silence afterward.”
* **Income:** monster bounties, relic recovery.
* **Relationships:** begrudging ally of Lucen (#278); often crosses paths with Darian (#277).
