## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born into a family of wealthy instrument makers, Airi rebelled from classic performance to fuse art with chemistry.
She calls her work “singing alchemy” — equal parts science and sparkle.

* **Lifestyle:** lavish, cluttered, full of mirrors and half-finished potions.
* **Income:** private commissions from nobles and performers.
* **Relationships:** flirts with both apprentices and teachers; rumored to have dated a noble client for “field research.”
