## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a war-cleric during the Severance Crusades, Mirra abandoned battlefields to found a chapel for unburied soldiers.
She now travels between ruins, performing rites for the forgotten dead.

* **Lifestyle:** monastic comfort — candles, tea, and ink.
* **Income:** donations, relic restorations.
* **Relationships:** regarded as a “holy mother” by her choir apprentices; secretly corresponded with Sir Nael in his early knight years.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Her compassion is quiet but absolute: she believes death is sacred, not feared.
