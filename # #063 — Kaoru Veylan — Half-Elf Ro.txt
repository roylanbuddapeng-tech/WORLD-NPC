## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Kaoru grew up a street performer with perfect pitch. When his illusions charmed the wrong noble, he was offered a new career: espionage through seduction and music. He now sells secrets by song.

* **Lifestyle:** Lavish but lonely — sleeps in perfumed silk, dines alone.
* **Income:** Espionage contracts and high-end performances.
* **Relationships:** Partner and occasional rival to Mina Soryu; flirtatious tension with Orin Kaelis.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Truth is a blade duller than charm; people reveal more when they smile.

* **Weaknesses:** Fragile pride, addicted to attention.
