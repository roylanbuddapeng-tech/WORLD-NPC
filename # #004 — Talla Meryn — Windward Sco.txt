## 📜 Background & Lifestyle**

* **Background:** Grew up riding desert couriers; lost her twin in a trade raid. Joined the Guard to prove survival can be an art.
* **Lifestyle:** Nomadic; sleeps under stars. Keeps a sketchbook of wind shapes.
* **Income:** Border scout pay + bounty for accurate wind reports.
* **Relationships:** On friendly terms with Rinna Vaelune; occasional training partner.

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics**

Values **independence over comfort**. Sees loyalty as chosen, not owed. Protects travelers from predators and corrupt guards alike.

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths**

* **Fears:** Getting lost in total stillness.
* **Weaknesses:** Prone to reckless solo chases.
* **Strengths:** High agility, sharp perception, adaptable under pressure.

## 💞 Interactions (Behavioral Style)**

“Tomboy Type 1” — light-hearted, feminine smile, but lively and teasing. Flicks sweat from her forehead and grins, “What, can’t keep up?” When flustered, she punches a shoulder instead of admitting it. Likes to make others blush by getting too close under the pretense of “adjusting your stance.”
