## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a court guard to the House of Glasswind, Ren left after refusing to execute a political rival.
Now he teaches nobles how to duel properly — and thieves how to make it look like art.

* **Lifestyle:** moderate, stays in inns with good wine and bad pianos.
* **Income:** tutoring, duels, private commissions.
* **Relationships:** flirtatious friendship with Rhea Corvalen, rivalry with Taro Wynthal.
