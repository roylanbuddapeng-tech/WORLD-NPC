## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Serene grew up as an apprentice to the Glass Gardens’ chief alchemist.
After a failed experiment that created sentient crystal vines, she set out to refine her craft through fieldwork.

* **Lifestyle:** keeps lab notes on her arm with chalk, lives in a dorm room full of glowing flasks.
* **Income:** potion sales and university grants.
* **Relationships:** occasionally assists Dr. Cassia (#201) with Echo-based mixtures.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She believes knowledge is a duty — even mistakes deserve to be written down for others to learn from.

* **Weaknesses:** low endurance; poor reflex in melee.
