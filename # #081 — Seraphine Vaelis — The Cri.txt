## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A courtesan-turned-ambassador whose wit and charm ended wars before they began. Once served the Archfey *Queen of Thorns*, who gifted her the “voice that compels truth.”

* **Lifestyle:** opulent but disciplined; candlelit studies, perfumed chambers.
* **Income:** embassy salary + private negotiation fees.
* **Relationships:** protective mentor to Lucan Edevane; rival flirtation with Diego Valcázar.
