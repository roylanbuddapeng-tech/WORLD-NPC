## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Support Focus)

* **Role:** **Buff/Debuff Maestro / Face**
* **Opener:** **Bardic Inspiration** to striker → *Faerie Fire* to enable team advantage.
* **Flow:** **Unsettling Words** before ally save-or-suck; *Silvery Barbs* to flip clutch rolls; *Healing Word* on falling allies.
* **Finisher:** *Calm Emotions* to shut down mobs or *Suggestion* to end a fight without blood.

**Effect Flavor:** her voice layers like a choir; silver notes spiral outward as feathered motes that perch on allies’ shoulders.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born to minor nobility, Ilyana spent years arbitrating duels with words.
Seeing treaties fail in the real world, she left the dais to carry her voice where it saves lives fastest.

* **Lifestyle:** tidy apartment above a rehearsal hall; tea, scores, sunrise warmups.
* **Income:** court retainers + field stipends.
* **Relationships:** friendly respect for Kaela Vorn (#218); duet partner with Selene Rhyne (#214) on night patrol rites.
