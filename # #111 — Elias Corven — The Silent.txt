## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A veteran soldier turned monster slayer, Elias learned silence as survival.
He wanders between contracts, chasing rumors of Echo-corrupted beasts.

* **Lifestyle:** solitary, minimalistic, sleeps with crossbow within arm’s reach.
* **Income:** monster bounties, debt collection, high-risk retrievals.
* **Relationships:** mentor-ally to Taro Vensai; mutual respect, no wasted words.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

He measures morality by consequence, not intention. “A clean kill is better than a merciful wound.”

* **Weaknesses:** Emotionally distant, haunted by past.
