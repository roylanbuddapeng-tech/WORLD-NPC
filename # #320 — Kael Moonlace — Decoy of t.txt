## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Backline Illusion Support / Advantage Engine)

* **Role:** **advantage battery / stealth enabler / controller**
* **Opener:** **Blessing of the Trickster** on the party striker → **Invoke Duplicity** to flank via decoy.
* **Flow:** **Mirror Image** on self; **Pass without Trace** for team vanish; **Hold Person** to pin a priority target.
* **Finisher:** decoy baits OA while Kael slides a **Command** that forces the enemy to expose a flank.

**Damage Flavor:** silver cords thrumm like harp strings; duplicates giggle quietly from the wrong direction.

* **Traits:** silky voice, playful gaze, expert at lingering too close; acts coy but is always three steps ahead.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Temple-runaway turned chapel trickster; he learned theology to circumvent it. Now he’s the smile that unlatches doors and the hymn that hides a footstep.

* **Lifestyle:** loft over a bell-maker’s shop, racks of masks and ribbons.
* **Income:** stealth escorts, “clean exits,” and prank-ritual fees.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

He believes kindness is misdirection’s goal: if someone must be fooled, let it be the cruel.
