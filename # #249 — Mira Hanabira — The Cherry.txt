## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

An orphan picked up by the Bloomspire guild, Mira turned out to be a natural prodigy at color mixing — and chaos.
She’s the reason the atelier now has *“splash-proof walls”*.

* **Lifestyle:** tiny shared dorm with shelves of pink bottles.
* **Income:** guild apprentice stipend + bonus from field work.
* **Relationships:** idolizes Nara (#247); flirts innocently with couriers for discounts.
