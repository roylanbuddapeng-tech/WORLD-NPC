## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Lane Striker / Heat Zoner)

* **Role:** **frontline striker / space control**
* **Opener:** **Rage** → slide in with **scimitar**; **Storm Aura (Desert)** pings a priority target for **1d6 fire** each round.
* **Flow:** herd foes along the rail with spear feints; go **Reckless** only to draw eyes while allies pass.
* **Finisher:** heel-pivot to the inside line, short cut under guard—heat haze snaps, fight ends.

**Damage Flavor:** heat shimmer beads along his blade; aura makes rain hiss to steam in tiny puffs around his ankles.
