## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born to a smith and a scribe, Renji fused craft and war into one calling. His innovations in runic warfare made him captain young — though he carries guilt for soldiers lost to his experiments.

* **Lifestyle:** Spartan efficiency; personal quarters lined with journals and blueprints.
* **Income:** Military pay + commission fees for spellsteel weaponry.
* **Relationships:** Mentor to Riko Hanamura; mutual respect with Meilin Arha (begrudgingly).

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes emotion must serve precision, not rule it. Values strength forged through discipline, but not cruelty.
