## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

An orphan from the floating temples, trained by spies who used songbirds as message carriers.
She developed her stealth around rhythm—each strike like a beat.

* **Lifestyle:** roams between jobs, sleeps wherever there’s height and wind.
* **Income:** assassination contracts, courier missions.
* **Relationships:** admires Vynessa (#284), friendly rivalry with Kael (#287).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Doesn’t kill for morality; kills for artistry.
She claims “ugly deaths make the air heavy.”
