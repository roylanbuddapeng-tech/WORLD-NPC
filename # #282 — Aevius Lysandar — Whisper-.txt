## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Infiltration / Control)

* **Role:** **social infiltrator / soft-control striker**
* **Opener:** **Disguise Self** → mingle → **Words of Terror** seed panic.
* **Flow:** **Silence** to isolate target → dirk with **Psychic Blades** → **Dissonant Whispers** to split formation.
* **Finisher:** whispers at the ear; if they run, trip line with wire fan, then vanish.

**Flavor:** his perfume sweetens, then turns cold—like night air over stone water—when he cuts.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Runaway choirboy who learned survival in dressing rooms and shadowed balconies.
He sells secrets to pay musicians’ debts and keeps the cruel rich entertained—until it’s time to ruin them.

* **Lifestyle:** silk sheets; sleeps by day behind sound curtains.
* **Income:** rumor brokerage, hush gigs, selective seductions.
* **Relationships:** friendly rivalry with Selyra (#281); rumored patronage by Seraphine (#270).
