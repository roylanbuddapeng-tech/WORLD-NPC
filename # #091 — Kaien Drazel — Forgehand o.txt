## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Former soldier turned smith. Kaien fought in the Ember Revolt, lost comrades, and rebuilt the guild from the ashes. His hammer forges both weapons and futures.

* **Lifestyle:** structured; up before dawn, ends day with ale and music.
* **Income:** blacksmith contracts, enchanted weapon commissions.
* **Relationships:** Mentor to younger smiths, quiet respect for Selene Arctis, occasional philosophical duels with Lioren Vale.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes that power means nothing unless it builds something after destruction.
