## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Control / Off-Healer / Attrition Caster**
* **Opener:** *Entangle* or *Spike Growth* to shape the field → activate **Symbiotic Entity**.
* **Flow:** kite enemies through hazardous ground; **Halo of Spores** chips on entries; patch allies with *Cure Wounds*.
* **Finisher:** **Blindness/Deafness** on priority target, then drag through *Spike Growth* with *Thorn Whip*.

**Damage Flavor:** pale-green motes swirl like silent bells; each hit releases a breath of forest-rot perfume.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Raised by fen-herbalists, Anzu documented how Echo surges alter fungal networks.
She adventurers to stop “bad blooms” before towns fall sick.

* **Lifestyle:** tidy shack on stilts; shelves of labeled jars.
* **Income:** field commissions, antidote sales.
* **Relationships:** trades cures with Seraphine (#219); night patrols with Selene (#214).
