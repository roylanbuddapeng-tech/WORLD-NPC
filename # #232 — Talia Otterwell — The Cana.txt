## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Support Focus)

* **Role:** **Buff/Control Healer**
* **Opener:** toss **Experimental Elixir (Resilience/Swiftness)** to frontline → *Faerie Fire* for party advantage.
* **Flow:** set **Grease** lanes, then kite enemies through while allies strike; pop **Aid** early.
* **Healing Line:** trigger **Healing Elixir** or *Cure Wounds*; when using **Flaming Sphere (M)**, add **+3** damage once/turn via **Alchemical Savant** to keep pressure while supporting.
* **Finisher:** crowd herd into choke and let **Flaming Sphere** and **Grease** control space while she body-blocks with glass shield (flavor).

**Effect Flavor:** her mixtures bloom into glowing bubbles that pop into scent-notes (mint for healing, citrus for speed, clove for courage).

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Dock-born otterkin who apprenticed under a canal apothecary; saved an entire chorus barge with a single batch of improvised stabilizers.
Now she prototypes “carryable miracles” for patrols.

* **Income:** city stipend + hazard bonuses + potion commissions.
