## 💬 Personality

* **Traits:** shy but determined, curious about death in a non-morbid way.
* **Ideal:** Peace — “To remember is to keep them alive.”
* **Bond:** guards a cemetery of children who died during the Echo Plague.
* **Flaw:** freezes when others die near her — it reminds her of her own rebirth.
* **Quirk:** whispers small goodbyes to insects before stepping on grass.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Liora was once a plague orphan resurrected by a cleric’s dying spell. Since then, she sees faint lights over the dead—souls waiting to be remembered.

* **Lifestyle:** simple, clean, organized; often hums when cleaning graves.
* **Income:** donations, guiding travelers through haunted paths.
* **Relationships:** student of Kaien (#269); admired by Seraphine (#270).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes all life and death deserve kindness; killing without remembrance is the true sin.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** total darkness; losing her light means reliving death.
* **Weaknesses:** fragile, emotionally attached to spirits.
* **Strengths:** potent against undead; her healing glows with necrotic inversion.
