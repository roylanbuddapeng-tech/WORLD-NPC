## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Tactical Dervish)

* **Role:** **Frontline Duelist / Counter-Fighter**
* **Opener:** spins her spear in *trip stance* to break the opponent’s footing.
* **Flow:** mixes feints and leaps; uses *Riposte* for punishing missed strikes.
* **Finisher:** leaps high, *Precision Attack* thrust straight down with “Raptor’s Fang.”

**Damage Flavor:** the spear glows gold mid-spin, leaving behind brief streaks like hawk feathers.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Raised among winged mercenaries, Rhea learned agility and showmanship from the arenas before joining the Pike Guild. She left after refusing to fix matches.

* **Lifestyle:** nomadic, loves festivals.
* **Income:** prize money, hunting contracts, teaching self-defense to travelers.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Strength is joy, not cruelty. She fights to remind people of what living *feels* like.
