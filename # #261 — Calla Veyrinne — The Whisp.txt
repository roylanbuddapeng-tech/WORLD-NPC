## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

The youngest daughter of the Veyrinne estate, Calla preferred plants to parties. She left home to learn “magic that makes things grow.”
Now works under Marielle (#258), studying the bond between sound, flora, and magic.

* **Lifestyle:** small cottage near arboretum, filled with flowers and glowing mushrooms.
* **Income:** sells enchanted seeds, healing herbs.
* **Relationships:** adored by locals, teased affectionately by her mentor and peers.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She believes all living things respond to kindness before command — a dangerous idea in a world of soldiers.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** loud explosions; hates fire spells but uses them anyway.
* **Weaknesses:** unpredictability; easily distracted by cute things.
* **Strengths:** cheerful energy boosts morale; natural bond with beasts and plants.
