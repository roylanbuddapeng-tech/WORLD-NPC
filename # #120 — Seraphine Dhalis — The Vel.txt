## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Seraphine was a noble tutor before war took her house.
She turned blade into art — creating the *Velvet Style*, a fusion of grace and violence.

* **Lifestyle:** opulent yet solitary; owns private training hall.
* **Income:** duel exhibitions, noble protection contracts.
* **Relationships:** mentor to Nerine Vaelith; former ally of Liora Venshade’s mother.
