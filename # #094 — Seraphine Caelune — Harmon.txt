## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born into the Thalassaran Silver Line, Seraphine rose from choir-guard to knight-captain. Trained others to hear the “song of steel” — the rhythm of combat and harmony of restraint.

* **Lifestyle:** methodical; prays before dawn, trains by twilight.
* **Income:** guard salary and temple stipend.
* **Relationships:** former instructor to Lyra Thalassara; mentors Meilin Zhou through letters.
