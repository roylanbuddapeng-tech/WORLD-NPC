## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

* **Background:** Born among temple attendants; her dolphin-kin bloodline awakened after surviving a shipwreck as a child. She believes the ocean spared her for a purpose.
* **Lifestyle:** Joyous and messy; sleeps on hammocks near open windows.
* **Income:** Temple stipend + sea-blessing offerings.
* **Relationships:** Looks up to Selune Varethiel as mentor; younger-sister bond with Nara Vehlis.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Life is water—never meant to be held still. She heals without judgment, even foes swept by darker tides.
