## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Kaenra is the daughter of a blacksmith who taught her to fight and forge equally.
Her hands bear burn scars from years of learning without magic gloves—she calls them her “honor marks.”

* **Lifestyle:** simple; sleeps in forge lofts, keeps an anvil charm for luck.
* **Income:** smithing commissions + adventuring contracts.
* **Relationships:** close to Sariel Kenzora; flirts lightly with Rin Tavel (to annoy him).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She believes anger is energy—waste it and you lose strength; forge it, and you shape destiny.

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** losing control of her inner fire, burning someone innocent.
* **Weaknesses:** easily provoked, refuses to back down.
* **Strengths:** speed, raw striking power, immunity to heat and panic.
