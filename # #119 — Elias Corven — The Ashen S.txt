## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born to librarians in Valeronnes, Elias grew up between shelves taller than houses.
His talent for fire evocation came when he accidentally melted his desk during an exam.
He now studies the moral weight of destructive magic.

* **Lifestyle:** disciplined but lonely; works late into candle hours.
* **Income:** research stipends, tutoring apprentices.
* **Relationships:** student partner to Liora Venshade; respects Talon Rhyss’s craft.
