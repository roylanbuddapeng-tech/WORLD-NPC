## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born a noble singer of House Vellorai, Seraphine discovered her voice drew spirits instead of applause.
She turned her gift into a conduit for restless souls, leading rituals that bring closure through sound.

* **Lifestyle:** luxurious, scented candles and feathered couches.
* **Income:** royal patronage and noble commissions.
* **Relationships:** admired by Velna (#268); rumored rival of Lyra (#1).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She believes beauty can sanctify death — that every loss deserves a melody to immortalize it.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** losing her voice; silence terrifies her more than ghosts.
* **Weaknesses:** emotionally vulnerable to flattery; overuses magic for aesthetics.
* **Strengths:** unmatched performance presence, wide-range AoE support, powerful necrotic resonance.
