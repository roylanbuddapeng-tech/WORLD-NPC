## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a noble lady married to a general, Mireia lost everything in a plague. Her grief drove her to necromancy, believing memory could be preserved through flesh and echo.
Now she wanders as a mercenary funeral mage, ensuring the dead are not left to the corruption of Severance.

* **Lifestyle:** candlelit manor suite, sleeps among quiet relics.
* **Income:** commissions from nobles to retrieve lost souls.
* **Relationships:** whispered partner of Dr. Cassia (#201); rumored rival of the Thalassaran priesthood.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She rejects divine authority — believes mortals must own death itself. To her, necromancy is devotion, not desecration.

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** her husband’s soul fading from the ring.
* **Weaknesses:** emotionally fragile when reminded of her past.
* **Strengths:** mastery over necrotic control and social manipulation.
