## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A scholarship kid who mapped canal plants by starlight, Yuna proved that resonance blooms follow celestial cycles.  
She joined adventurers to test her models under real pressure.

- **Lifestyle:** tidy bunk; dried flowers pressed in every book.  
- **Income:** academy stipend + expedition shares.  
- **Relationships:** shares data with Rei Solenne; debates politely with Eira Valenne on tactics.
