## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born to a diplomat father and a siren mother, Miraen was trained to speak beautifully and lie elegantly. His half-aquatic charm makes him equally at home in council halls and coral reefs.

* **Lifestyle:** Refined; enjoys music, poetry, and sea-bathing rituals.
* **Income:** Diplomatic stipend, commission bonuses, gifts from admirers.
* **Relationships:** Professional tension with Elior Fenn; secret fondness for Lyra Thalassara’s stoicism.
