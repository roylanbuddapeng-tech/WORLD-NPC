## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Heiress-turned-instructor who left court politicking for the academy stage.
She mentors street-talented fighters into performers who survive the crowd *and* the blade.

* **Lifestyle:** refined townhouse; practice room full of mirrors and ribbons.
* **Income:** academy stipend, noble commissions, private recitals.
* **Relationships:** friendly sparring with Seraphine Dhalis; cordial rivalry with Ren Kaizel.

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics
