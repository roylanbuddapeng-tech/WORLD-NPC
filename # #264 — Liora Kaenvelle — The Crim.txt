## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Liora once guarded caravans across scorched lands before being saved by a fiendish spirit who offered her power in return for her promise to “burn brighter than despair.”
Now she’s a freelancer mercenary and field protector for smaller towns.

* **Lifestyle:** lives above a forge, always smells faintly of smoke.
* **Income:** contracts, demon hunts, magical item retrievals.
* **Relationships:** childhood friend of Calla (#261); sparring rival to Yuri (#263).
