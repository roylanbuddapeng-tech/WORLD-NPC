## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born in the smoldering city of Skarnov, Talon grew up forging before he could read.
After losing his wife to a forge accident caused by an Echo surge, he turned toward battlecraft — seeking control over chaos through engineering.

* **Lifestyle:** ascetic but structured; keeps forge spotless.
* **Income:** commissions for guild, artifact contracts, weapon restoration.
* **Relationships:** mutual respect with Ren Kaizel; often forges gear for adventurers of Thal-Rune.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Values creation over destruction but accepts that steel must sometimes spill blood to protect what it builds.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Losing control of Korr, or seeing his work twisted for evil.
* **Weaknesses:** Emotionally guarded; guilt-laden perfectionism.
* **Strengths:** Engineering genius, mental fortitude, battle resilience.
