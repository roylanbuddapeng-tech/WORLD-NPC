* **Role:** **Mid-range Support Summoner / Controller**
* **Opener:** *Find Familiar* (Raven scout) → *Mage Armor* → *Animate Dead* on fallen foes.
* **Flow:** directs undead as silent flankers while focusing fire with *Toll the Dead*.
* **Finisher:** *Mirror Image* to evade, repositions while undead close in.

**Flavor:** his undead glow faint blue from eye sockets, moving like shadows synchronized with his gestures.

## 💬 Personality

* **Traits:** calm, intellectual, curious about moral limits of necromancy.
* **Ideal:** Knowledge must not fear death — “The end of life is only the beginning of memory.”
* **Bond:** his familiar, **Kael**, carries souls he cannot yet release.
* **Flaw:** detached; speaks of people as “data” when emotional distance sets in.
* **Quirk:** writes notes mid-conversation with ink made of ash.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born to a noble scholar and a temple archivist.
Eryndor rejected divine magic, choosing instead to preserve memories through necromancy.
He sees the undead as “living libraries” of history that deserve curation, not destruction.

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist apartment, one candle always burning for his dead students.
* **Income:** commissions from noble houses seeking ancestral contact.
* **Relationships:** philosophical acquaintance of Mireia (#266), academic rival to Dr. Cassia (#201).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes that memory must outlive morality — necromancy is merely a preservation of truth.

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** the day his familiar refuses to obey — meaning Kael’s soul has remembered too much.
* **Weaknesses:** low social instinct, emotionally withdrawn.
* **Strengths:** precision control of undead, tactical intellect.
