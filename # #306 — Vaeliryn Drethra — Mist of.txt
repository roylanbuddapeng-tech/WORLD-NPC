## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born among Drow exiles who sought redemption through art; Vaeliryn’s empathy made him dangerous. He joined the Loom to master the art of desire’s balance—making others feel too much before realizing it.

* **Lifestyle:** luxury suite filled with incense and satin.
* **Income:** noble entertainment, charm consultations, enchantment commission.
* **Relationships:** linked emotionally to Aurelia (#302); rivals in seduction.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

He doesn’t see manipulation as wrong—only poor craft if it leaves scars. “A perfect charm heals what it touches.”
