* **Background:** Former free-diver turned arena champion, now teaches disciplined combat to young adventurers. Once saved a caravan by fighting underwater during an Echo wave.

* **Income:** Training fees + coastal defense wages.
* **Relationships:** Friends with Selune Varethiel; sometimes rivals with Rinna Vaelune in sparring.
