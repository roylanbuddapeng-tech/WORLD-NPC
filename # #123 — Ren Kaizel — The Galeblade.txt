## 💬 Personality

* **Traits:** Dry humor, pragmatic, protective of subordinates.
* **Ideal:** Freedom — “Every man should choose the storm he walks through.”

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Ren once commanded the elite “Azure Vanguard” until they were betrayed during the Severance riots.
Now he leads a smaller crew, taking jobs for survival — not glory.

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist; sleeps wherever the wind allows.
* **Income:** mercenary pay, dueling contracts.
* **Relationships:** rival to Miyu Hanari, mentor to younger mercenaries.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Believes honor is a story people tell after war — he values results and those who can bear them.
