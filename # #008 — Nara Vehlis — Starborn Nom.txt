* **Background:** Born to traveling nomads; trained under a wandering monk who taught her to “listen to sand.” Protects caravans from raiders and Echo storms.
* **Lifestyle:** Lives from pack to pack; sleeps under stars; writes poems on bones she finds in the dunes.
* **Income:** Caravan guard pay + monastic donations.
* **Relationships:** Drinks tea with Talla Meryn whenever their paths cross.

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics**
