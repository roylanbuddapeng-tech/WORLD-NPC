## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Frontline Bruiser / Disruptor**
* **Opener:** **Reckless Attack** + **Rage**, crash the line with greatsword sweep.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Raised as a ceremonial breaker in a fringe cult, Kira learned to destroy cursed icons.
She escaped with help from mercs and now sells that same skill to protect towns from relic-plagues.

* **Lifestyle:** camp-born; sleeps near the door, blade across knees.
* **Income:** hazard pay + relic disposal bounties.
* **Relationships:** drinking buddy with Nara Veylan (#207); arm-wrestling rival to Kaori Namue (#203).

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Better to crack a false god than kneel to it. Smash, then help pick up the pieces.
