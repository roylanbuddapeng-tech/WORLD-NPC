## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a temple dancer chosen to channel divine rhythm, Amara discovered her healing was strongest when she moved with the pulse of life itself.
Now she trains younger clerics to “heal with tempo,” turning pain into performance.

* **Lifestyle:** clean quarters with practice mats, warm tea, and soft chime music.
* **Income:** temple stipend, ceremonial contracts, donation shares.
* **Relationships:** often coordinates with Catalina Nocturne (#238); mentor of novice Hina Sorelle (#216).

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics
