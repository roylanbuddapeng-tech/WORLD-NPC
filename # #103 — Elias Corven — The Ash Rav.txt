## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a decorated city guard, Elias left after refusing orders that cost innocent lives. He rebuilt himself as a sellsword who fights only for causes worth the blood spilled.

* **Lifestyle:** minimalist; lives near his forge.
* **Income:** contract hunts, weapon commissions.
* **Relationships:** protective mentor toward Ryn Kael’veris; unspoken mutual trust with Lyra Thalassara.
