📜 Background & Lifestyle

Background: Daughter of a low-born baker and a monastery gardener; showed innate resonance healing as a child. Chose the field corps to “heal where hymns can’t reach.”

Lifestyle: Communal dorm living, minimal luxury; shares her pay with orphans.

Income: Chapel stipend + field healing commissions.

Relationships: Friendly rival of Neris Thalassa (race courier); trusted by the Azure choir.

🧩 Morals & Ethics

Kindness is duty. Believes mercy is the most precise weapon. Refuses to judge those who sin out of fear.

😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

Fears: Failing to save a friend; corruption of sacred water.

Weaknesses: Overextends healing too early; underestimates physical exhaustion.

Strengths: High composure, natural leadership, radiant healing focus.

💞 Interactions (Behavioral Style)

“รุ่นเพื่อน” tone — light teasing and open laughter. She pats comrades’ shoulders mid-battle with “Stay up, I’ll cover you!” Loves flustering stoic adventurers by winking mid-prayer. In calm times, jokes about “charging by smile” for healings.

🤫 Secrets & Plot Hooks

Secret: Once channeled divine energy through forbidden resonance—still bears a faint mark on her ribs.

Hooks:

The Azure Pulse: Escort Celin into Echo-contaminated catacombs to restore sacred resonance wells.

Chorale of Bones: A corrupted choir sings beneath Valeronnes—Celin insists she can purify them.

The Step Unheard: Retrieve her lost anklet bead rumored to be the key to a forgotten healing hymn.
