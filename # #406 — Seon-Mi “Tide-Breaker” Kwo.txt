## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Ritual Enforcer / Fear Engine)

* **Role:** **frontline controller / single-target eraser**
* **Opener:** **Dreadful Aspect** to shatter nerve lines, then **Command: “Kneel.”**
* **Flow:** tag leader with **Inflict Wounds** (2nd-level slot for bite), finish with **Divine Smite** on a longsword cut; **Hellish Rebuke** punishes anyone who marks her.
* **Finisher:** shield-press against the rail and a quiet **“Hush.”** Rain draws a blue line toward safety—everyone but her target gets to live.

**Damage Flavor:** every Smite turns rain to needle-spray around the blade, like surf caught in moonlight.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Dockside acolyte who learned prayers that sound like weather reports. She keeps a ledger of “debts to the tide,” written in tidy columns beside recipes for fish stew.

* **Lifestyle:** narrow room above a net-mender’s shop; immaculate armor rack, shell stickers in a tin.
* **Income:** hush alms, paid “calmings,” anonymous dock tithes.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Order is mercy. Mercy demands blood—**but exactly the right amount**.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths

* **Fears:** Severance spans where rain makes **no surf sound**—her rites feel fake.
* **Weaknesses:** few spell slots; speed tied to medium armor; ranged focus fire can stall her approach.
* **Strengths:** **AC 19**, fear aura (**Dreadful Aspect**), **Control Undead**, **big single-target burst** via **Inflict Wounds + Smite**.

---

## 💞 Interactions (Behavioral Style)

She places a shell sticker on your scabbard, smiling like a patient teacher:

> “Be still. I’ll pay the tide—**not** with you.”

---

## 🤫 Secrets & Plot Hooks

1. **Debt Ledger:** names for “next calm sea” include a PC’s ally—find who forged the entry.
2. **False Surf:** a Severance cell sells **silence stones** that nullify her rites; crack the ring.
3. **Black Tithe:** an undead crew stalks the docks—Seon-Mi wants to “hire” them via **Control Undead** for one awful night.
