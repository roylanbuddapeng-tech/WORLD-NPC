## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Once a battlefield illusionist, Lucien turned to teaching to atone for deaths caused by his own deceptive tactics.
Now he uses illusions to inspire wonder instead of fear.

* **Lifestyle:** private study filled with mirrors and wine glasses.
* **Income:** lecture fees, magical shows.
* **Relationships:** quiet friendship with Selena Veyra; professional rivalry with Kaien Dross.
