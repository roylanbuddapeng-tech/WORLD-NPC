## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Support / Control First)

* **Role:** **Protector / De-escalation Support**
* **Opener:** **Emissary of Peace** → *Sanctuary* on a focus ally → **Protection** reactions to peel threats.
* **Flow:** *Bless* early; use **Rebuke the Violent** to punish big hits; **Shield of Faith** on the frontliner.
* **Nonlethal:** calls for surrender; uses blunt-longside strikes; *Sleep* to end skirmishes fast.
* **Finisher:** **Divine Smite** only when life is clearly at risk.

**Damage Flavor:** ripples of pale-blue light cascade across her shield; every parry leaves a soft bell ring that calms tempers.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Raised by bridge-wardens, Rei broke a cycle of violent reprisals by negotiating a gang’s surrender with nothing but a shield and a prayer.
Now she’s the first call when tempers flare.

* **Lifestyle:** shared barracks room, neat bunk, tea tins labeled like orders.
* **Income:** city retainer + hazard bonuses + private guard contracts.
* **Relationships:** works alongside Lucia Ashvale (#239); often requests healing teams led by Mariel (#234).
