## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Cassian was once a lecturer at the Brass Academy, until an experiment tore his lab apart — and half his students vanished into an Echo rift.
Now he studies how to make the same mistake *safely.*

* **Lifestyle:** neat, minimalist apartment lined with notes.
* **Income:** research grants, tutoring, occasional field work.
* **Relationships:** fatherly toward Kael; professionally wary of Aria Sunveil’s faith.

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics
