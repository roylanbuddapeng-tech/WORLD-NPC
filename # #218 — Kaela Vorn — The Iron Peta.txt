## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A veteran of the canal wars, Kaela refused noble retirement to train the next generation.
Her leadership stabilized an entire battalion after a Severance outbreak.

* **Lifestyle:** ascetic luxury — owns little, maintains immaculate order.
* **Income:** royal guard stipend + tactical consultancy.
* **Relationships:** mentor figure to younger adventurers like Selene (#214).

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

Law serves compassion, not pride. She enforces order to protect hope, not to bind it.
