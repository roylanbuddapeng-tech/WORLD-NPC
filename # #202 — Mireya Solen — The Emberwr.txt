## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Daughter of a noble smith line, she defied expectations by joining soldiers instead of managing forges.
Now she refines weapons for fieldwork — and personally tests each one in combat.

* **Lifestyle:** workshop-centric, lives above her forge.
* **Income:** weapons commissions + adventuring shares.
* **Relationships:** mentor to Kaen Vos; friendly rivalry with Yuna Vareth (#201).
