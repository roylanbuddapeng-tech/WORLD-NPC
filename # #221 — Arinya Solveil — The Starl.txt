## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Raised by astronomers in the Vale, Arinya believes fate can be decoded like a constellation.
Her youthful fascination makes her both brilliant and reckless, charting magic storms alone at night.

* **Lifestyle:** scholarly simplicity; sleeps surrounded by scrolls and star charts.
* **Income:** observatory stipend + rare star reading commissions.
* **Relationships:** student of Professor Maelis (#206); close friend to Mira Thaless (#212).

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She believes destiny isn’t unchangeable—only *visible.* If you can read it, you can write it back.
