## 🎯 Combat Tactics

* **Role:** **Mobile Striker / Scout**
* **Tactics:** Opens from shadows, hits fast, disengages to confuse enemies. Uses *Pass without Trace* for infiltration and *Flurry of Blows* for disruption.
* **Signature Opener:** Spins mid-air, strikes with twin blades, vanishes into a swirl of blue petals.
* **Finisher:** Leaps from darkness, performs a crescent kick glowing faintly silver.
* **Damage Flavor:** Impacts cause soft ripples of light, like reflections in water.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Born in a fishing town near Thalassara’s harbor; joined the monastery after saving a monk from bandits. She dreams of becoming a “Resonant Dancer” — warrior who fights through rhythm.

* **Lifestyle:** Minimalist but cozy; collects feathers and small crystals.
* **Income:** Courier pay + temple allowance.
* **Relationships:** Admires Liora Vaentis like an older sister; playfully argues with Kaena Mistral about “cool poses.”

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics

She believes intent matters more than perfection; compassion first, technique second.
