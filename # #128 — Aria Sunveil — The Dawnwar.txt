## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

A temple prodigy, Aria trained among the Dawn Choir to merge art and faith through resonance chants.
After a vision of the Severance blighting the sun’s echo, she began traveling to heal those scarred by it.

* **Lifestyle:** ascetic but warm; keeps her quarters fragrant with candles.
* **Income:** temple donations, pilgrim aid.
* **Relationships:** mentor to Liora; admired secretly by Ren Kaizel.
