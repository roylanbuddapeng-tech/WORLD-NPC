## 🎯 Combat Tactics (Support / Control)

* **Role:** **Initiative Fixer / Save-Doctor / Soft Controller**
* **Opener:** call **Portent** to rig an enemy save (bad roll) or ally clutch check (good roll).
* **Flow:** *Protection from Evil and Good* on frontline vs. aberrations; *Enhance Ability* for key skill scenes; *Web* to halt rushes.
* **Team Play:** *Message* for silent coordination; *See Invisibility* to counter ambushers.
* **Finisher:** a “Portent fail” into allies’ hold or smite; she then *Invisibility* to reposition.

**Effect Flavor:** her stylus sketches glowing circles; the chosen d20 result briefly spins above her glasses like a halo clock-hand.

## 📜 Background & Lifestyle

Dock-born to archivists, Aoi learned to read people before books.
Her first “prophecy” saved a refugee cart from an Echo surge — she simply saw the pattern and moved first.

* **Lifestyle:** tiny loft full of pinned omen cards and kettle tea.
* **Income:** conservatory stipend, hazard bonuses, private “exam proctor” gigs (spotting cheaters with Portent—ethically, she warns first).
* **Relationships:** friendly rivalry with Lucia Ashvale (#239); study buddy of Iria Fenwright (#228).
