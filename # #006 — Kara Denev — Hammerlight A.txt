## 📜 Background & Lifestyle**

* **Background:** Raised in the heat of Skarnov’s forges. Apprenticed to a weapon master who fell during an Echo breach. Took his hammer and vowed to finish his masterpiece.
* **Lifestyle:** Lives in workshop dorms; drinks like a dwarf, fights like one too.
* **Income:** Smithing contracts + gladiatorial exhibitions.
* **Relationships:** Friends with Rinna Vaelune; sometimes spars with her over “form vs. force.”

---

## 🧩 Morals & Ethics**

Believes discipline is sacred. Strength must be built, not gifted. Hates using “resonance” shortcuts in combat—prefers honest sweat and metal.

---

## 😨 Fears / Weaknesses / Strengths**

* **Fears:** Becoming too weak to forge.
* **Weaknesses:** Blunt personality; struggles with subtlety.
* **Strengths:** High durability, great morale anchor for allies.

---

## 💞 Interactions (Behavioral Style)**

“Tomboy Type 2” — rowdy, physically affectionate. Smacks comrades on the back and laughs: “That bruise means you’re learning!” When embarrassed, she crosses her arms and grumbles. Likes to challenge others to arm wrestling instead of chatting.

---

## 🤫 Secrets & Plot Hooks**

* **Secret:** Keeps a fragment of the cracked master anvil—when struck, it hums faintly in lost tongues.
* **Hooks:**

  1. **Forgefire Echo:** Protect Kara while she tempers a blade inside a semi-corrupted forge.
  2. **The Broken Anvil:** Recover her master’s soul trapped within an Echo-imbued ingot.
  3. **Hammerlight Showdown:** Duel Kara to prove your mettle; she forges you a weapon if you impress her.
